Our computations for $G=\operatorname{GL}_2$ explicitly describe images of geometric constant term functors when restricted to a distinguished point inside $\mathrm{Bun}_{T_{\{b\}}}$, where $T_{\{b\}}$ is the split inner form of the Levi quotient of the corresponding parabolic subgroup $P_b$.

### [5] [Estimating spectral radius of Bell-type operator via finite dimensional approximation of orthogonal projections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.10939)
*Yuki Fujii,Toyohiro Tsurumaru*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文研究了量子力学中两个正交投影算子的交换子谱半径估计问题，该问题与贝尔-CHSH算子的谱半径确定等价。作者提出了用有限维子空间上的正交投影来近似的方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子力学中两个正交投影算子交换子的谱半径估计问题，该问题与贝尔-CHSH算子的谱半径确定密切相关，在量子信息理论中具有重要意义。

Method: 通过有限维子空间上的正交投影来近似无限维希尔伯特空间上的正交投影，从而估计交换子的谱半径。

Result: 该方法提供了交换子谱半径的上界和下界，当投影算子的矩阵表示具有"恒定角度单移位形式"时，这些界是精确的。

Conclusion: 提出的近似方法能够有效估计正交投影算子交换子的谱半径，在特定条件下可以得到精确结果，为解决量子力学中的相关问题提供了有效工具。

Abstract: In quantum mechanics, the following problem is important: For two orthogonal projections P and Q on a separable infinite dimensional Hilbert space V, estimate the spectral radius of the commutator [A,B], where A=2P-I, B=2Q-I.   To approach this problem, we give a method to approximate orthogonal projections on V by those on finite dimensional subspaces.
  This method provides an upper bound and a lower bound for the spectral radius of [A,B], which become exact when the matrix representations of P and Q are "constant-angle one-shifted form".

### [6] [Uniform bounds and uncertainty for asymptotics of representations of $p$-adic ${\rm GL}_N$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.11063)

TL;DR: 本文证明了关于p进GL_N表示在主要同余子群下固定向量维数增长的两个独立结果：一是基于GK维度的固定向量增长统一界，二是酉表示中GK维度与矩阵系数衰减率之间的定量关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究p进GL_N表示在主要同余子群下固定向量维数的增长行为，旨在建立统一的界定量关系，深化对表示理论中维度增长和矩阵系数衰减的理解。

Method: 在Langlands和Zelevinsky分类框架下，通过分析表示在主要同余子群下的行为，利用GK维度概念，建立固定向量维数增长的统一界，并扩展至Harish-Chandra-Howe系数。

Result: 证明了固定向量维数增长的统一界，该界仅依赖于表示的GK维度；对于酉表示，建立了GK维度与矩阵系数衰减率之间的定量关系。

Abstract: We prove two results on the growth of dimensions of fixed vectors of representations $π$ of $p$-adic ${\rm GL}_N$ under principal congruence subgroups: First, a uniform bound on the growth of fixed vectors in terms of the GK-dimension $π$, which we extend to a uniform bound on the Harish-Chandra--Howe coefficients. Second, for $π$ unitary, a quantitative relationship between the GK-dimension of $π$ and the rate of decay of its matrix coefficients. These results are independent of one another and proved in the framework of the Langlands and Zelevinsky classifications.
