### [1] [Dual Affine Robinson-Schensted Correspondence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20383)
*Daoji Huang,Sylvester W. Zhang*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文引入对偶仿射Robinson-Schensted对应，建立了扩展仿射对称群与满足兼容条件的元组之间的双射，推广了经典Robinson-Schensted对应的Fomin生长图和Viennot阴影线构造，并参数化了仿射A型Kazhdan-Lusztig胞腔。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典Robinson-Schensted对应推广到仿射对称群情形，建立扩展仿射对称群与组合对象之间的双射，并用于参数化Kazhdan-Lusztig胞腔。

Method: 推广Fomin的生长图和Viennot的阴影线构造，引入对偶仿射Robinson-Schensted对应；通过与仿射矩阵球构造及Shi对应的对偶性建立联系，利用仿射evacuation关联Q-tabloid。

Result: 给出了扩展仿射对称群与元组(\bar{P},\bar{Q},λ,N)之间的双射，参数化了仿射A型Kazhdan-Lusztig胞腔。

Conclusion: 构造的生长图可能具有仿射旗之间相对位置的几何实现，类似于Steinberg和van Leeuwen在经典情形中的解释。

Abstract: We introduce the dual affine Robinson-Schensted correspondence that gives a bijection between the extended affine symmetric group and tuples $(\bar{P},\bar{Q},λ,N)$, where $\bar{P}$ and $\bar{Q}$ are tabloids, $λ$ is a partition, and $N$ is an integer, subject to compatibility conditions. The construction generalizes Fomin's growth diagrams and Viennot's shadow lines for the classical Robinson-Schensted correspondence on the symmetric group, and is dual to the affine matrix ball construction as well as Shi's correspondence, in the sense that the $P$-tabloids are the same, and the $Q$-tabloids are related by affine evacuation. As a consequence, our construction also parametrizes Kazhdan-Lusztig cells in affine type $A$. We conjecture that the growth diagrams we construct admit a natural geometric realization in terms of relative positions of affine flags, similar to the interpretation given by Steinberg and van Leeuwen in the classical case.

### [2] [A combinatorial approach to scattering diagrams](https://arxiv.org/abs/1806.05094)
*Nathan Reading*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文利用簇散射图与簇代数之间的联系，计算了仿射型秩-2簇散射图极限墙的函数，并在斜对称仿射情形下恢复了Reineke公式，同时发现符号Narayana数的生成函数扮演了类似簇变量的角色。在无圈有限型中，通过Cambrian扇和可排序元素构造了簇散射图并给出直接证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 散射图源于镜像对称，而簇散射图被开发用于证明簇代数的关键结构结果。作者希望利用这一联系来计算特定类型散射图的极限墙函数，并探索其与组合对象（如Narayana数）的关系。

Method: 利用簇散射图与簇代数之间的已知联系，对仿射型秩-2簇散射图的极限墙函数进行计算；在无圈有限型情形，借助Cambrian扇和可排序元素构造簇散射图，并给出简洁的直接证明。

Result: 在斜对称秩-2仿射情形下恢复了Reineke公式；发现符号Narayana数的生成函数在类似簇变量的角色中出现；在无圈有限型中成功构造了簇散射图。

Conclusion: 簇散射图与簇代数的联系可用于计算极限墙函数，并揭示与组合结构（如Narayana数）的深刻关联；此外，无圈有限型情形可通过Cambrian扇和可排序元素简洁构造。

Abstract: Scattering diagrams arose in the context of mirror symmetry, but a special class of scattering diagrams (the cluster scattering diagrams) were recently developed to prove key structural results on cluster algebras. We use the connection to cluster algebras to calculate the function attached to the limiting wall of a rank-2 cluster scattering diagram of affine type. In the skew-symmetric rank-2 affine case, this recovers a formula due to Reineke. In the same case, we show that the generating function for signed Narayana numbers appears in a role analogous to a cluster variable. In acyclic finite type, we construct cluster scattering diagrams of acyclic finite type from Cambrian fans and sortable elements, with a simple direct proof.

</details>

### [3] [Bridging 4D QFTs and 2D VOAs via 3D high-temperature EFTs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.18130)
*Arash Arabi Ardehali,Mykola Dedushenko,Dongmin Gang,Mikhail Litvinov*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文研究了4d 𝒩=2超共形场论在高温极限下（尤其是高页片）的性质，通过对(A₁,A₂ₙ) Argyres-Douglas理论进行r-twisted圆约化，在第二页片上得到了Gang-Kim-Stubbs系列的3d 𝒩=2超对称增强的rank-0理论，其边界支持Virasoro最小模型VOA M(2,2n+3)。拓扑扭曲后得到由M(2,2n+3)模张量范畴控制的非幺正TQFT，而其他页片给出其幺正或非幺正的Galois共轭。这为建立4d 𝒩=2 SCFT与2d VOA之间的对应关系提供了新的3d有效场论视角。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索4d 𝒩=2超共形场论在高温极限下高页片的普适信息，特别是与r-twisted圆约化产生的3d拓扑量子场论（TQFT）之间的关系，并建立与2d VOA（顶点算子代数）的联系。

Method: 应用Di Pietro-Komargodski型超对称有效场论技术，对(A₁,A₂ₙ) Argyres-Douglas理论进行r-twisted圆约化，利用其Maruyoshi-Song拉格朗日量（具有显式𝒩=1超对称性），分析高温极限在不同页片上的行为。

Result: 在第二页片上得到Gang-Kim-Stubbs系列的3d 𝒩=2超对称增强rank-0理论，边界支持M(2,2n+3) Virasoro最小模型VOA；拓扑扭曲后产生非幺正TQFT。其他页片给出这些TQFT的幺正或非幺正Galois共轭。

Conclusion: 该工作打开了从四超荷视角理解4d 𝒩=2 SCFT与2d VOA之间著名对应关系的新途径，提供了3d SUSY增强的系统方法，并为与4d 𝒩=2 SCFT相关的各种MTC的Galois轨道提供了3d QFT视角。

Abstract: The high-temperature limit of the superconformal index, especially on higher sheets, often captures useful universal information about a theory. In 4d $\mathcal{N}=2$ superconformal field theories with fractional r-charges, there exists a special notion of high-temperature limit on higher sheets that captures data of three-dimensional topological quantum field theories arising from r-twisted circle reduction. These TQFTs are closely tied with the VOA of the 4d SCFT. We study such high-temperature limits. More specifically, we apply Di~Pietro-Komargodski type supersymmetric effective field theory techniques to r-twisted circle reductions of $(A_1,A_{2n})$ Argyres-Douglas theories, leveraging their Maruyoshi-Song Lagrangian with manifest $\mathcal{N}=1$ supersymmetry. The result on the second sheet is the Gang-Kim-Stubbs family of 3d $\mathcal{N}=2$ SUSY enhancing rank-$0$ theories with monopole superpotentials, whose boundary supports the Virasoro minimal model VOAs $M(2,2n+3)$. Upon topological twist, they give non-unitary TQFTs controlled by the $M(2,2n+3)$ modular tensor category (MTC). The high-temperature limit on other sheets yields their unitary or non-unitary Galois conjugates. This opens up the prospect of a broader four-supercharge perspective on the celebrated correspondence between 4d $\mathcal{N}=2$ SCFTs and 2d VOAs via interpolating 3d EFTs. Several byproducts follow, including a systematic approach to 3d SUSY enhancement from 4d SUSY enhancement, and a 3d QFT handle on Galois orbits of various MTCs associated with 4d $\mathcal{N}=2$ SCFTs.

</details>
