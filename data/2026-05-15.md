### [1] [A twist on ring morphisms and crepant contractions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.24030)
*Marina Godinho*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文构造了环同态上的扭曲函子，证明其在Frobenius正合范畴下是导出自等价，并揭示了Donovan-Wemyss的非交换扭曲实际上是标量限制函子上的球面扭曲，进而为奇异概形构造了新的球面扭曲。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 统一和理解环同态诱导的扭曲构造，并将其与球面扭曲理论联系起来，同时将相关理论推广到更一般的奇异概形情形。

Method: 利用Frobenius正合范畴的导出等价理论，构造环同态上的扭曲函子，并通过理论推导证明其与Donovan-Wemyss非交换扭曲及球面扭曲的关系。

Result: 证明了环同态扭曲是导出自等价；揭示了Donovan-Wemyss的非交换扭曲本质上是标量限制函子上的球面扭曲；为奇异概形构造了新的球面扭曲实例。

Conclusion: 该工作统一了环同态扭曲与球面扭曲理论，为研究更一般奇异概形上的扭曲构造提供了新工具，并扩展了关于收缩形变诱导球面扭曲的已有成果。

Abstract: Given a ring morphism, this paper constructs the twist functor around the induced derived restriction of scalars functor. We prove that the twist around ring morphisms is a derived autoequivalence in the setting of twists induced by Frobenius exact categories. As a corollary, it is shown that the noncommutative twist introduced by Donovan and Wemyss is in fact a spherical twist around the restriction of scalars functor. We then use this technology to obtain new spherical twists for singular schemes, and discuss how our result extends previous works on spherical twists induced by crepant contractions.

### [2] [Diagrammatic technique for Vogel's universality](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12911)
*D. Khudoteplov,A. Sleptsov*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文复兴了Vogel基于Λ-代数的图论方法，展示了其在李代数通用计算中的有效性，并提供了大量实例分析。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Vogel于1999年提出了通用李代数的假设，尽管该假设尚未解决，但李理论中的许多量具有通用描述。然而，Vogel的图论方法在过去二十多年中未受到足够关注，本文旨在复兴这一被忽视的技术。

Method: 本文复兴并应用了Vogel提出的Λ-代数图论方法，通过大量实例展示了该方法在通用计算中的能力。

Result: 研究表明，Vogel的图论方法确实能够实现真正通用的计算，并提供了多个示例予以佐证。

Conclusion: Vogel的Λ-代数图论方法具有重要价值，值得重新关注和深入研究。

Abstract: In his 1999 preprint "Universal Lie Algebra", P. Vogel put forward a hypothesis on the existence of a universal Lie algebra. Although this hypothesis remains open, it is known that many quantities in Lie theory admit universal descriptions. Remarkably, almost all such universal formulas have been obtained through the representation theory of simple Lie (super)algebras, whereas Vogel's original framework was based on a more abstract diagrammatic algebra. Nevertheless, the diagrammatic approach has received little attention over the past two decades, since the last contributions by P. Vogel and J. Kneissler.
  In this work, we revive the diagrammatic technique grounded in Vogel's $Λ$-algebra and show that it enables truly universal computations. We examine numerous examples and discuss them.

</details>

### [3] [Sphericalization and the Universal Spherical Adjunction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.15037)
*Fernando Abellán,Jonte Gödicke*

Main category: math.CT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为稳定∞-范畴的伴随（或局部稳定(∞,2)-范畴）提供一种反转扭曲和余扭曲函子的通用简单方法，并借此研究球面伴随的结构性质。

Method: 通过给出反转扭曲和余扭曲函子的显式构造，进而构造球面伴随范畴到所有伴随范畴的包含函子的左右伴随，并用这些伴随函子描述行走球面伴随范畴（一种局部稳定(∞,2)-范畴）的结构。

Conclusion: 本文提供的方法不仅统一了反转扭曲/余扭曲函子的构造，还为球面伴随的分类和球面函子的无限伴随性质提供了简洁的合成证明。

Abstract: For every adjunction of stable $\infty$-categories -- or more generally, in any locally stable $(\infty,2)$-category -- we give a simple procedure for inverting the twist and cotwist functors associated to this adjunction. As a consequence, we obtain an explicit construction for a left and right adjoint to the inclusion of the $(\infty,2)$-category of spherical adjunctions of stable $\infty$-categories into all adjunctions. We utilize these adjoints to give a description of the walking spherical adjunction, a locally stable $(\infty,2)$-category which classifies spherical adjunctions, and to provide a synthetic proof of the fact that every spherical functor admits infinitely many left and right adjoints.

Method: 通过球函子周围的扭曲构造导出自等价，利用商映射p: A→B上的自然条件，证明伴随的cotwist是B的Nakayama函子的平移，并结合局部到整体技术。

### [8] [Linear representations of manifolds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14013)
*Rongbiao Thomas Wang,Lek-Heng Lim,Ke Ye*

Main category: math.DG

TL;DR: 本文提出了G-流形的线性表示概念，将其推广到无群结构的流形上，并利用该概念给出了Mostow-Palais等变嵌入的有效界，包括显式维数和构造方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 群或代数的线性表示将抽象元素编码为矩阵，但许多流形不具备群结构。作者希望将这种表示思想推广到任意G-流形，并解决Mostow-Palais等变嵌入缺乏有效界的问题。

Method: 将G-流形的线性表示定义为从流形到矩阵空间的映射，使得G-作用被编码为矩阵乘法。利用这种表示，为Mostow-Palais等变嵌入构造显式的、维数最小的嵌入。

Result: 给出了Mostow-Palais等变嵌入中目标G-模V的显式维数，证明了这些界是紧致的，并提供了最小维数嵌入的显式表达式。

Conclusion: 该工作填补了Mostow-Palais嵌入缺乏有效界的空白，提供了一种构造性方法，且所得界是最优的。

Abstract: A finite-dimensional linear representation of a group or an algebra may be regarded as a map into a space of matrices, endowing abstract elements with coordinates, and encoding algebraic operations as matrix products. With this in mind, we define a linear representation of a $\mathsf{G}$-manifold $\mathcal{M} $ as a map into a space of matrices, representing points as matrices and the $\mathsf{G}$-action as matrix products. We show that this generalizes group representations to any $\mathsf{G}$-manifold that may not have a group structure, with homogeneous spaces $\mathsf{G}/\mathsf{H}$ an important special case; and in this case it also generalizes Cartan embeddings of symmetric spaces to more general $\mathsf{G}/\mathsf{H}$. To demonstrate the utility of such manifold representations, we use them to provide effective bounds for Mostow-Palais $\mathsf{G}$-equivariant embeddings of $\mathsf{G}$-manifolds into $\mathsf{G}$-modules $\mathbb{V}$. Unlike Whitney and Nash embeddings, Mostow-Palais embeddings have no known effective bounds; before our work, it was only known that $\dim \mathbb{V} < \infty$ if $\mathsf{G}$ is compact. We will give explicit values for $\dim \mathbb{V}$ and show that our bounds are sharp. Furthermore, our method is constructive, giving explicit expressions for these minimal-dimensional Mostow-Palais embeddings.

</details>
