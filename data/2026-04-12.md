### [1] [L-modules are mixed](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07719)
*Leslie Saper*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了局部对称空间X的L模是混合的，可以表示为带权上同调L模的迭代映射锥，并证明了在排除某些类型时，交上同调与带权上同调同构。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究局部对称空间X的L模的结构性质，特别是它们与带权上同调的关系，以及交上同调与带权上同调之间的同构关系。

Method: 使用L模作为组合模型，分析其在约化Borel-Serre紧化上的构造性层复形。通过弱微支集这一可计算的局部不变量，将L模表示为带权上同调L模的迭代映射锥。

Result: 证明了任何L模都是混合的，即可以表示为带权上同调L模的迭代映射锥。这些带权上同调"构建块"由弱微支集中的不可约正则L_P-模V索引。在排除D、E、F类型时，交上同调与带权上同调同构。

Conclusion: L模具有混合结构，可以通过带权上同调模块分解。交上同调与带权上同调在大多数情况下同构，这为理解局部对称空间的拓扑和几何性质提供了新的视角。

Abstract: Let X be the locally symmetric space associated to a reductive $\mathbb Q$-group G and an arithmetic subgroup $Γ$. An L-module M is a combinatorial model of a constructible complex of sheaves on $\widehat X$, the reductive Borel-Serre compactification of X whose strata $X_P$ are indexed by $Γ$-conjugacy classes of parabolic $\mathbb Q$-subgroups P of G. We show that any L-module M is "mixed" in the sense it is an iterated mapping cone of maps to or from shifted weighted cohomology L-modules on strata $X_P$ of $\widehat X$ with coefficients in V, an irreducible regular $L_P$-module. These weighted cohomology "building blocks" are indexed (up to multiplicity) by V in the weak micro-support of M which is a computable local invariant. As an application we prove that the intersection cohomology of $\widehat X$ is isomorphic to the weighted cohomology of $\widehat X$, at least excluding $\mathbb Q$-types D, E, and F.

### [2] [Lifting banal representations of classical groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07516)
*Johannes Droschl*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了对于非阿基米德局部域上的辛群或分裂正交群，当素数ℓ满足"banal"条件时，所有光滑不可约的𝔽̄ℓ-表示都可以提升到ℚ̄ℓ-表示，并将结果推广到更一般的经典群，应用包括在强banal情况下证明辛-正交或酉对偶对的Howe对偶性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究非阿基米德局部域上经典群的模表示理论，特别是当特征ℓ满足"banal"条件时，探讨𝔽̄ℓ-表示能否提升到ℚ̄ℓ-表示的问题，这对于理解模表示与特征零表示之间的关系具有重要意义。

Method: 定义"banal"素数概念（即不整除群在剩余域k上点数的素数），然后证明对于满足该条件的素数ℓ，所有光滑不可约的𝔽̄ℓ-表示都存在到ℚ̄ℓ-表示的提升。方法可能涉及模表示理论、p进群表示论以及经典群的表示理论。

Result: 证明了对于任意banal素数ℓ，辛群或分裂正交群上的任何光滑不可约𝔽̄ℓ-表示都可以提升到ℚ̄ℓ-表示。类似结果也推广到更一般的辛型、正交型或酉型经典群。作为应用，在强banal情况下证明了辛-正交或酉对偶对的Howe对偶性。

Conclusion: 该论文建立了在banal素数条件下，经典群的模表示可以提升到特征零表示的重要结果，这为研究模表示与特征零表示之间的关系提供了理论基础，并成功应用于证明Howe对偶性，推动了p进群表示理论的发展。

A prime $\ell$ is called banal with respect to $\mathrm{G}$ if it does not divide the cardinality of the $k$-points of $\mathrm{G}$, where $k$ is the residue field of $\mathrm{F}$. In this paper we show that for every banal prime $\ell$, any smooth irreducible $\overline{\mathbb{F}}_\ell$-representation of $\mathrm{G}(\mathrm{F})$ admits a lift to $\overline{\mathbb{Q}}_\ell$. We also state similar results for more general classical groups of symplectic, orthogonal or unitary type. As an application we prove Howe-duality in the strongly banal case for symplectic-orthogonal or unitary dual pairs.

### [5] [Local Lie Theory in Quasi-Banach Lie Algebras: Convergence of the BCH Series and Geometric Implications](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.08265)
*Nassim Athmouni,Mohsen Ben Abdallah,Mondher Damak,Marwa Ennaceur,Amel Jadlaoui,Lotfi Souden*

Main category: math.FA

TL;DR: 本文为配备拟范数的李代数建立了局部李理论，证明了在连续性常数有限的情况下，Baker-Campbell-Hausdorff级数在原点邻域收敛，并分析了拟范数指数p对几何变形的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究配备拟范数的李代数的局部李理论，这类结构出现在弱Schatten理想和某些Hardy空间算子代数等应用中，需要建立相应的收敛理论。

Method: 利用Aoki-Rolewicz定理构造等价的p-范数，在完备拟度量空间中通过柯西序列论证证明BCH级数收敛，并分析拟范数指数p对几何变形的影响。

Result: 证明了BCH级数在原点邻域收敛，收敛半径与连续性常数相关；数值计算显示经典组合界在代数抵消情况下保守，实际收敛半径更大；拟范数指数p改变度量性质但不影响李代数结构。

Conclusion: 为拟范数李代数建立了完整的局部李理论，包括BCH收敛性、局部李群结构和几何变形分析，为弱Schatten理想等应用提供了理论基础。

Abstract: We develop a local Lie theory for Lie algebras equipped with a quasi-norm, i.e., complete topological vector spaces satisfying a relaxed triangle inequality $\|x+y\|\le \Ctri(\|x\|+\|y\|)$ with $\Ctri\ge 1$. We prove that the Baker--Campbell--Hausdorff (BCH) series converges in a neighborhood of the origin, provided the quasi-norm admits a continuous Lie bracket with finite continuity constant $\Cbracket$. The proof relies on the Aoki--Rolewicz theorem to construct an equivalent $p$-norm satisfying $p$-subadditivity, enabling rigorous Cauchy-sequence arguments in the complete quasi-metric space $(E, d_p)$. This yields a well-defined local Lie group structure via the exponential map. We analyze the geometric deformation induced by the quasi-norm exponent $p\in(0,1]$, showing that it modifies metric properties while preserving the underlying Lie algebraic structure. Numerical estimates of BCH coefficients up to degree $20$, with coefficients defined precisely via Hall--Lyndon basis projection, demonstrate that classical combinatorial bounds are conservative in the presence of algebraic cancellations, allowing significantly larger practical convergence radii in structured algebras. Applications include weak Schatten ideals $\mathcal{L}_{p,\infty}(H)$ for $0<p<1$ and certain Hardy-space operator algebras.
  \smallskip\noindent\textbf{Remark on the convergence radius.} The Catalan-majorant method yields convergence for $\|x\|+\|y\| < 1/(4\Cbracket)$; the additional factor $\Ctri$ appearing in the combined constant $\Ctotal = \Ctri\Cbracket$ is an artefact of switching to the $p$-norm to establish Cauchyness of partial sums. When the quasi-norm itself is directly a $p$-norm ($\Ctri=1$), no such penalty arises and the radius reduces to $1/(4\Cbracket)$.
