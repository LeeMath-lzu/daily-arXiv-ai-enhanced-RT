### [1] [Graded Unitarity in the SCFT/VOA Correspondence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.23781)
*Arash Arabi Ardehali,Christopher Beem,Madalena Lemos,Leonardo Rastelli*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一种称为"分级幺正性"的顶点代数新概念，用于描述四维N=2超共形场论对应的顶点代数结构，并初步分类了满足此性质的Virasoro和仿射Kac-Moody顶点代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 四维N=2超共形场论对应的顶点代数继承了四维理论的幺正性，但不同于传统顶点代数的幺正概念。需要建立一种新的幺正性框架来准确描述这种对应关系。

Method: 提出并定义"分级幺正性"概念，研究其在Virasoro和仿射Kac-Moody顶点代数中的实现。在特定自然假设下，分析哪些中心电荷和能级满足分级幺正性条件。

Result: 发现只有(2,p)中心电荷的Virasoro顶点代数和边界容许能级的sl2、sl3仿射Kac-Moody顶点代数可能满足分级幺正性，这些恰好是已知从四维理论中出现的顶点代数。

Conclusion: 分级幺正性成功捕捉了四维幺正性在顶点代数对应中的后果，为分类这类顶点代数提供了理论基础，并揭示了与四维理论的深刻联系。

Abstract: Vertex algebras that arise from four-dimensional, $\mathcal{N}=2$ superconformal field theories inherit a collection of novel structural properties from their four-dimensional ancestors. Crucially, when the parent SCFT is unitary, the corresponding vertex algebra is not unitary in the conventional sense. In this paper, we motivate and define a generalized notion of unitarity for vertex algebras that we call \emph{graded unitarity}, and which captures the consequences of four-dimensional unitarity under this correspondence. We also take the first steps towards a classification program for graded-unitary vertex algebras whose underlying vertex algebras are Virasoro or affine Kac--Moody vertex algebras. Remarkably, under certain natural assumptions about the $\mathfrak{R}$-filtration for these vertex algebras, we show that only the $(2,p)$ central charges for Virasoro VOAs and boundary admissible levels for $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ and $\mathfrak{sl}_3$ Kac--Moody vertex algebras can possibly be compatible with graded unitarity. These are precisely the cases of these vertex algebras that are known to arise from four dimensions.

### [2] [Fractionally Calabi-Yau algebras and cluster tilting](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19582)
*Aaron Chan,Osamu Iyama,Rene Marczinzik*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文建立了扭曲分数Calabi-Yau代数与高Auslander代数之间的等价关系，为高表示有限代数提供了新的构造方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究扭曲分数Calabi-Yau代数与高Auslander代数之间的联系，为高Auslander-Reiten理论提供新的视角，并构造大类别的高Auslander代数和高表示有限代数。

Method: 通过证明有限整体维数代数A是扭曲分数Calabi-Yau的当且仅当存在i使得复制代数A^{(i)}是高Auslander代数，建立两类代数之间的等价关系。应用这一结果研究d-表示有限代数上d-簇倾斜模的稳定自同态代数。

Result: 证明了有限整体维数扭曲分数Calabi-Yau代数类等于d-表示有限代数上d-簇倾斜模的稳定自同态代数类。给出了扭曲n/2-Calabi-Yau代数的显式刻画，并建立了扭曲分数Calabi-Yau代数有界导出范畴与相关高预投射代数的Z-分次稳定模范畴之间的三角等价。

Conclusion: 该研究建立了高Auslander-Reiten理论与扭曲分数Calabi-Yau代数之间的新联系，为构造高Auslander代数和高表示有限代数提供了系统方法，深化了对这两类代数结构的理解。

Abstract: We show that the class of twisted fractionally Calabi-Yau algebras of finite global dimension coincides with the stable endomorphism algebras of $d$-cluster tilting modules over $d$-representation-finite algebras. This is an application of our main result stating that an algebra $A$ of finite global dimension is twisted fractionally Calabi-Yau if and only if there exists $i$ such that the replicated algebra $A^{(i)}$ is a higher Auslander algebra if and only if there exist infinitely many $i$ such that $A^{(i)}$ is a higher Auslander algebra. This gives a new connection between the study of higher Auslander-Reiten theory and twisted fractionally Calabi-Yau algebras, and provides a new construction of large classes of higher Auslander algebras and higher representation-finite algebras. We give several applications such as an explicit characterisation of twisted $\frac{n}{2}$-Calabi-Yau algebras, and a triangle equivalence between the bounded derived category of a twisted fractionally Calabi-Yau algebra of finite global dimension and the $\mathbb{Z}$-graded stable module category of an associated higher preprojective algebra.

### [3] [Computing the Cousin-Zuckerman Resolution and the Lusztig-Vogan Bijection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19554)
*Jack A. Cook*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文证明了旗流形上标准D-模整体截面的特征化定理，该结果结合了Langlands分类和Knapp-Zuckerman分类，并用于计算平凡表示的Cousin-Zuckerman分解，以及在GL(n,ℍ)中证明Lusztig-Vogan双射和计算最低K-型映射。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 通过证明旗流形上标准D-模整体截面的特征化定理，将Langlands分类与Knapp-Zuckerman分类相结合，然后应用该结果计算平凡表示的Cousin-Zuckerman分解，并在GL(n,ℍ)的特殊情况下证明Lusztig-Vogan双射和计算最低K-型映射。

Conclusion: 本文填补了文献中关于旗流形上标准D-模整体截面特征化的空白，建立了连接Langlands分类和Knapp-Zuckerman分类的桥梁，为实约化群表示理论提供了新的计算工具和理论框架，特别是在GL(n,ℍ)中取得了具体的分类结果。

Abstract: The goal of this article is to give a proof of a result seemingly absent from the literature characterizing global sections of standard $\mathcal{D}$-modules on the flag variety. This characterization yields a mixture of the Langlands Classification of admissible representations with the Knapp-Zuckerman classification of tempered representations of a real reductive group. We use this result to compute the Cousin-Zuckerman resolution of the trivial representation in terms of standard $(\mathfrak{g},K)$-modules. Further, in the case of $GL(n,\mathbb{H})$ we use this to prove the Lusztig-Vogan bijection for $n=2,3$ and compute the lowest $K$-type map for the zero and principal orbits for general $n$ as well as the image of the trivial representation for even orbits.

TL;DR: 该论文为orthosymplectic李超代数$\mathfrak{spo}(4|1)$的有限维不可约表示定义了Verma向量系统，并证明其线性无关性，从而构成表示空间的一组基，称为$\mathfrak{spo}(4|1)$的Verma基。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为orthosymplectic李超代数$\mathfrak{spo}(4|1)$的有限维不可约表示构造类似于$\mathfrak{sp}_4$表示中Verma基的基向量系统，以更好地理解和研究这类超代数表示的结构。

Result: 证明了Verma向量系统是线性无关的，从而构成了具有最高权$λ$的$\mathfrak{spo}(4|1)$有限维不可约表示$L(λ)$的一组基，称为$\mathfrak{spo}(4|1)$的Verma基。

We then show the linearly independence of this vector system. It turns out to be a basis of the finite dimensional irreducible representation $L(λ)$ of the orthosymplectic Lie superalgebra $\mathfrak{spo}(4|1)$ with the highest weight $λ,$ which analogs to the Verma basis of representations of $\mathfrak{sp}_4,$ called the Verma basis of the finite dimensional irreducible representation of $\mathfrak{spo}(4|1)$.
