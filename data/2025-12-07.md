*Matthew Pressland*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 该论文证明正矩阵簇坐标环上的源标记和目标标记簇代数结构是准重合的，解决了Muller和Speyer的猜想


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 正矩阵簇通常具有多个簇代数结构，其中源标记和目标标记结构是最被理解的两种。Muller和Speyer在2017年提出猜想，认为这两种结构是准重合的，需要证明这一猜想

Method: 使用范畴化方法：作者对相关簇代数结构进行范畴化，与Çanakçı和King合作对完美匹配和扭曲进行范畴化，并利用Fraser和Keller的簇代数准等价理论

Result: 证明了源标记和目标标记簇代数结构准重合，即一个结构中的簇变量可以表示为另一个结构中的簇变量与冻结变量的Laurent单项式的乘积。同时证明了Muller和Speyer的左扭曲映射是从目标标记结构到源标记结构的准簇等价

Conclusion: 成功解决了Muller和Speyer的猜想，为正矩阵簇的簇代数理论提供了重要进展，展示了范畴化方法在簇代数研究中的强大作用

Abstract: By work of a number of authors, beginning with Scott and culminating with Galashin and Lam, the coordinate rings of positroid varieties in the Grassmannian carry cluster algebra structures. In this article, we show that these two cluster algebra structures quasi-coincide, meaning in particular that a cluster variable in one structure may be expressed in the other structure as the product of a cluster variable and a Laurent monomial in the frozen variables. This resolves a conjecture attributed to Muller and Speyer from 2017. The proof depends critically on categorification: of the relevant cluster algebra structures by the author, of perfect matchings and twists by the author with Çanakçı and King, and of quasi-equivalences of cluster algebras by Fraser and Keller. By similar techniques, we also show that Muller and Speyer's left twist map is a quasi-cluster equivalence from the target-labelled structure to the source-labelled structure.
