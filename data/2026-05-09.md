### [1] [Stable Invariants of Words from Random Matrices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.17733)
*Doron Puder,Yotam Shomroni,Danielle Ernst-West,Matan Seidel*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 本文形式化定义了从词随机矩阵中产生的新不变量，回答了一些情况下（涉及广义对称群）的问题，并提出了许多其他情况下的详细猜想。这些不变量包括Wilton最近定义的两个不变量：稳定本原秩和scl的非定向类比。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索从词随机矩阵（如随机置换、随机正交矩阵等）中产生的新不变量是否新颖、有趣，以及它们是否像scl（稳定交换子长度）那样具有内在的拓扑描述。

Method: 形式化定义来自词随机矩阵的不变量，在涉及广义对称群的情况下给出答案，并对其他情况提出猜想。

Result: 在广义对称群相关情形下给出了明确答案，发现这些不变量确实与Wilton定义的稳定本原秩和非定向scl等拓扑不变量相关。

Conclusion: 这些从词随机矩阵中衍生的不变量是新颖且有意义的，它们与拓扑、组合和代数不变量紧密相连，为scl的研究提供了新的视角和推广方向。

Abstract: Let $w$ be a word in a free group. A few years ago, Magee and the first named author discovered that the stable commutator length (scl) of $w$, a well-known topological invariant, can also be defined in terms of certain Fourier coefficients of $w$-random unitary matrices [arXiv:1802.04862]. But the random-matrix side of this equality can be naturally tweaked by considering $w$-random permutations, $w$-random orthogonal matrices and so on, to produce new invariants for any given word. Are these invariants new? interesting? Do they admit an intrinsic topological description as in the case of $w$-random unitaries and scl?
  The current paper formalizes the definition of these invariants coming from $w$-random matrices, answers the above questions in certain cases involving generalized symmetric groups, and poses detailed conjectures in many others. In particular, we present a plethora of topological, combinatorial and algebraic invariants of words which play, or are at least conjectured to play, a similar role to the one played by scl in the above-mentioned result. Among others, these invariants include two invariants recently defined by Wilton [arXiv:2210.09853]: the stable primitivity rank and a non-oriented analog of scl.

### [2] [Equivariant version of the characteristic quasi-polynomials of root systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05695)
*Ryo Uchiumi*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种等变特征拟多项式，它是Coxeter排列模q补集上置换角色的拟多项式，是根系统传统特征拟多项式的细化。论文证明了该多项式的若干等变K理论性质，对所有不可约既约根系进行了显式计算，并讨论了与扩展Dynkin图折叠构造的根系之间的关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统特征拟多项式无法捕捉根系统在模q补集上的置换角色信息，需要一种更精细的等变版本以揭示更深层的结构性质。

Method: 引入等变特征拟多项式作为传统特征拟多项式的细化，利用等变K理论工具建立相关性质，并对所有不可约既约根系进行显式计算，同时借助扩展Dynkin图折叠的方法讨论不同根系间的联系。

Result: 成功建立了等变特征拟多项式在等变K理论框架下的若干性质，给出了所有不可约既约根系的显式表达式，并揭示了其与扩展Dynkin图折叠构造的根系之间的内在联系。

Conclusion: 等变特征拟多项式是对传统特征拟多项式的有效细化，能够更全面地刻画根系统的结构信息，且与Dynkin图折叠构造存在深刻关联。

Abstract: An equivariant characteristic quasi-polynomial is a quasi-polynomial in $q$ consisting of the permutation character on the mod $q$ complement of the corresponding Coxeter arrangement. This concept is a refinement of the conventional characteristic quasi-polynomials of root systems. In this paper, we will show equivariant-theoretic refinements of the some properties of characteristic quasi-polynomials of root systems. Furthermore, we will explicitly compute equivariant characteristic quasi-polynomials of all irreducible reduced root systems and discuss the relationship with root systems constructed by the folding of the extended Dynkin diagrams.

### [3] [The Galois Alperin weight conjecture for finite category algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06166)
*Xin Huang*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文定义了有限范畴的Galois Alperin权猜想（GAWC），并将其约化为有限群的情形；对于EI-范畴，进一步给出了分块版本的猜想（BGAWC）及其约化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将经典的Galois Alperin权猜想从有限群推广到有限范畴代数，建立更一般的表示论框架。

Method: 利用Linckelmann定义的p-轨道范畴，构造Γ×Aut(𝒞)-等变双射，并通过约化方法将有限范畴的情形归结为有限群的情形。

Result: 成功将GAWC从有限范畴约化到有限群；对EI-范畴，给出了基于块的分区，并建立了BGAWC及其约化。

Conclusion: 有限范畴的Galois Alperin权猜想可通过约化到有限群来处理，为范畴表示论中的权理论提供了新路径。

Abstract: Let $p$ be a prime, $k$ an algebraic closure of $\mathbb{F}_p$ and $Γ$ the Galois group ${\rm Gal}(k/\mathbb{F}_p)$. Let $\mathcal{C}$ be a finite category and $\mathcal{O}_{\mathcal{C}}$ the $p$-orbit category of $\mathcal{C}$ defined by Linckelmann. We formulate a version of the Galois Alperin weight conjecture (GAWC) for finite category algebras stating that there exists a $Γ\times {\rm Aut}(\mathcal{C})$-equivariant bijection between the set of isomorphism classes of simple $k\mathcal{C}$-modules and that of the weights of $k\mathcal{O}_{\mathcal{C}}$. We reduce the GAWC for finite categories to finite groups. For $\mathcal{C}$ an EI-category, we give a partition of weights of $k\mathcal{O}_{\mathcal{C}}$ with respect to blocks of $k\mathcal{C}$ and then formulate a blockwise Galois Alperin weight conjecture (BGAWC) for $\mathcal{C}$. Similarly, we reduce the BGAWC for finite EI-categories to finite groups.

### [7] [Even-carry polynomials and cohomology of line bundles on the incidence correspondence in positive characteristic](https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.04166)
*Evan M. O'Dorney*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文针对特征p下一般超曲面X⊂ℙ^{n-1}×ℙ^{n-1}（即关联对应）上线丛的上同调群展开研究。对于n=3的情形，通过推广Nim多项式为"偶进位多项式"，解决了Liu–Gao–Raicu的递归关系，得到了显式公式；并对一般n,p的上同调形式提出了猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 特征0下关联对应线丛上同调已完全理解，但特征p下的上同调依赖于度(d,e)的p进制数字，性质复杂。Gao和Raicu（继Linyuan Liu之后）给出了n=3情形的递归描述，但缺乏显式解。

Method: 本文推广了Nim多项式，定义了"偶进位多项式"（even-carry polynomials），以此求解Liu–Gao–Raicu的递归关系，得到n=3且一般素数p下的显式上同调公式。

Result: 得到了n=3、一般素数p下关联对应线丛上同调的显式公式。此外，对一般的n和p提出了上同调形式的猜想。

Conclusion: 偶进位多项式成功解决了特定情形的递归问题；一般情形的猜想为后续研究提供了方向。

Abstract: We consider the cohomology groups of line bundles $\mathcal{L}$ on the \emph{incidence correspondence}, that is, a general hypersurface $X \subset \mathbb{P}^{n-1} \times \mathbb{P}^{n-1}$ of degrees $(1,1)$. Whereas the characteristic $0$ situation is completely understood, the cohomology in characteristic $p$ depends in a mysterious way on the base-$p$ digits of the degrees $(d, e)$ of $\mathcal{L}$. Gao and Raicu (following Linyuan Liu) prove a recursive description of the cohomology for $n = 3$, which relates to Nim polynomials when $p = 2$. In this paper, we devise a suitable generalization of Nim polynomials, which we call \emph{even-carry polynomials,} by which we can solve the recurrence of Liu--Gao--Raicu to yield an explicit formula for the cohomology for $n = 3$ and general $p$. We also make some conjectures on the general form of the cohomology for general $n$ and $p$, for which a recurrence relation was recently derived by Kyomuhangi--Marangone--Raicu--Reed.

</details>
