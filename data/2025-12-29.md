### [1] [Representing in Low Rank I: conjugacy, topological and homological aspects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.22052)
*Robynn Corveleyn,Geoffrey Janssens,Doryan Temmerman*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文系列研究有限群的低次不可约表示（n≤2）在数域上的性质，特别关注SL₂(O)算术秩为1的情况。第一部分从两个角度展开：一是用Serre同调良性质、小虚拟上同调维度和Klein型嵌入刻画表示空间；二是研究群环单位群中有限子群在不可约表示下的共轭类，涉及Zassenhaus猜想和子群同构问题的分块变体。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究有限群在数域上的低次不可约表示（特别是n≤2的情况）的性质，这些表示与算术秩为1的SL₂(O)结构相关。旨在理解这类表示空间的刻画特征，以及群环单位群中有限子群在表示下的行为。

Method: 1. 使用Serre的同调良性质、小虚拟上同调维度和高阶Klein型嵌入来刻画表示空间；2. 研究群环单位群中有限子群在不可约表示下的共轭类，引入Zassenhaus猜想和子群同构问题的分块变体；3. 分析高阶模群的同余子群及其对同余核的影响。

Result: 1. 获得了具有所述形式不可约表示的有限群的多个刻画，特别是证明了这类群恰好是那些其群环单位群可以从几乎映射到非阿贝尔自由群的群构造出来的群；2. 获得了群环单位群同余核的新信息；3. 对低秩表示下的Zassenhaus猜想和子群同构问题做出了贡献。

Conclusion: 该研究建立了有限群低次不可约表示与算术性质之间的深刻联系，为理解群表示与群环单位群结构提供了新视角，并为Zassenhaus猜想等相关问题在低秩情况下的研究奠定了基础。

Abstract: In this series of papers, we investigate properties of a finite group which are determined by its low degree irreducible representations over a number field $F$, i.e. its representations on matrix rings $\operatorname{M}_n(D)$ with $n \leq 2$. In particular we focus on representations on $\operatorname{M}_2(D)$ where $D$ is a division algebra having an order $\mathcal{O}$ such that $\mathcal{O}$ has finitely many units, i.e. such that $\operatorname{SL}_2(\mathcal{O})$ has arithmetic rank $1$. In this first part, the focus is on two aspects.
  One aspect concerns characterisations of such representing spaces in terms of Serre's homological goodness property, small virtual cohomological dimension and higher Kleinian-type embeddings. As an application, we obtain several characterisations of the finite groups $G$ whose irreducible representations are of the mentioned form. In particular, such groups $G$ are precisely those such that $\mathcal{U}(R G)$, with $R$ the ring of integers of $F$, can be constructed from groups which virtually map onto a non-abelian free group. Along the way we investigate the latter property for congruence subgroups of higher modular groups and its implications for the congruence kernel. This is used to obtain new information on the congruence kernel of the unit group of a group ring.
  The second aspect concerns the conjugacy classes of the images of finite subgroups of $\mathcal{U}(R G)$ under the irreducible representations of $G$. More precisely, we initiate the study of a blockwise variant of the Zassenhaus conjectures and the subgroup isomorphism problem. Moreover, we contribute to them for the low rank representations above.

### [5] [Fano compactifications of mutation algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21839)
*Joshua Enwright,Luca Francone,Joaquín Moraga,Hunter Spink*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 该论文引入了突变半群代数的概念，它同时推广了丛代数和半群代数，并研究了其谱的log Fano紧化性质，以及丛型簇与Cox环作为突变半群代数的等价关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了统一丛代数和半群代数的理论框架，探索代数几何、表示论和组合学中的新结构，并研究Fano簇的紧化问题。

Method: 引入突变半群代数的新概念，在奇点条件假设下构造谱的log Fano紧化，证明Q-因子klt Fano簇为丛型当且仅当其Cox环是Cl(X)-分次突变半群代数，并通过具体例子验证理论。

Result: 证明了突变半群代数的谱在温和奇点条件下存在log Fano紧化，且当U为Q-因子时紧化X也可选为Q-因子log Fano簇；建立了Q-因子klt Fano簇为丛型与Cox环为突变半群代数的等价关系。

Abstract: In this article, we introduce the notion of mutation semigroup algebras. This concept simultaneously generalizes cluster algebras and semigroup algebras. The compactification $X$ can be chosen to be a $\mathbb{Q}$-factorial log Fano variety whenever $U$ is $\mathbb{Q}$-factorial. Furthermore, we prove that a $\mathbb{Q}$-factorial klt Fano variety $X$ is of cluster type if and only if its Cox ring ${\rm Cox}(X)$ is a ${\rm Cl}(X)$-graded mutation semigroup algebra. In order to enlighten the previous theorems, we provide several explicit examples motivated by birational geometry, representation theory, and combinatorics.

### [6] [Skein and cluster algebras of punctured surfaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.15037)
*Enhan Li*

Main category: math.AC

TL;DR: 本文证明了带标记的曲面Σ上SL₂局部系统的A-簇变种满足Fock-Goncharov猜想，即标记骨架代数等于上簇代数，并建立了装饰Teichmüller空间的量子化与Roger-Yang骨架代数的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究曲面上的簇代数结构与Teichmüller空间量子化之间的联系，特别是验证Fock-Goncharov猜想在SL₂局部系统情形下的完全成立。

Method: 引入局部化簇变种作为装饰Teichmüller空间的代数版本，通过几何刻画证明标记骨架代数与上簇代数的等价性，并建立与Roger-Yang骨架代数的对应关系。

Result: 完全证明了带至少2个穿孔的三角化曲面上的SL₂局部系统的Fock-Goncharov猜想；标记骨架代数等于上簇代数；局部化簇变种的全局截面等于经典Roger-Yang骨架代数；标记骨架代数和经典Roger-Yang骨架代数具有正规性和Gorenstein性质；上簇代数有限生成。

Conclusion: 本文建立了曲面簇代数与Teichmüller空间量子化之间的深刻联系，完全解决了SL₂情形下的Fock-Goncharov猜想，为相关几何结构的研究提供了新的代数框架和量子化工具。

Abstract: We prove the full Fock--Goncharov conjecture for $\mathcal{A}_{SL_2,Σ_{g,p}}$, the $\mathcal{A}$-cluster variety of the moduli of decorated twisted $SL_2$-local systems on triangulable surfaces $Σ_{g,p}$ with at least 2 punctures. Equivalently, we show that the tagged skein algebra $Sk^{ta}(Σ)$, or the middle cluster algebra $\mathrm{mid}(\mathcal{A})$, coincides with the upper cluster algebra $U(Σ)$. Inspired by the work of Shen--Sun--Weng, we introduce the localized cluster variety $\mathring{\mathcal{A}}$ as the algebraic version of the decorated Teichmüller space $\mathcal{T}^d(Σ)$. We show its global section $Γ(\mathring{\mathcal{A}},\mathcal{O}_{\mathring{\mathcal{A}}})$ equals the classical Roger--Yang skein algebra $Sk^{RY}_{q\to1}(Σ)$, thereby providing a quantization of $\mathcal{T}^d(Σ)$ in terms of the Roger--Yang skein algebra $Sk^{RY}_q(Σ)$. As a consequence of our geometric characterizations, we deduce normality and the Gorenstein property of the tagged skein algebra $Sk^{ta}(Σ)$ and the classical Roger--Yang skein algebra $Sk^{RY}_{q\to1}(Σ)$, as well as finite generation of upper cluster algebra $U(Σ)$.
