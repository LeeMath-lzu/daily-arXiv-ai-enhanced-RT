### [2] [Open-Closed Hochschild Homology and the Relative Disk Mapping Space](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.05010)
*Yi Wang,Hang Yuan*

Main category: math.AT

TL;DR: 本文开发了陈氏模型的相对版本，为光滑流形之间的映射构造了相对圆盘映射空间的迭代积分模型，推广了陈氏关于自由环路空间的经典定理。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 陈氏通过迭代积分理论证明了自由环路空间的微分形式dga模型由Hochschild链复形给出。本文旨在发展这一模型的相对版本，考虑流形间光滑映射的相对圆盘映射空间。

Method: 通过自然关联于映射f的开闭同伦代数(OCHA)和开闭Hochschild同调理论，构造了相对圆盘映射空间的迭代积分模型。

Result: 主要定理表明，当M可缩或2-连通且具有奇数维球面的有理同伦型，N单连通时，所得映射是拟同构。

Conclusion: 该结果推广了陈氏关于自由环路空间的经典定理，并在上述特殊情况下扩展了Getzler-Jones关于双环路空间的定理。

Abstract: It is known that a model for the differential graded algebra (dga) of
differential forms on the free loop space $LN$ of a simply connected smooth
manifold $N$ is given by the Hochschild chain complex of the dga $\Omega(N)$ of
differential forms on $N$, as shown by K.-T. Chen via his theory of iterated
integrals. We develop a relative version of Chen's model. Given a smooth map
$f\colon N\to M$ between smooth manifolds, we consider the ``relative disk
mapping space'' consisting of pairs $(\Phi,\gamma)$ of maps $\Phi\colon \mathbb
D\to M$ and $\gamma\colon S^1\to N$ such that $\Phi|_{\partial\mathbb
D}=f\circ\gamma$. We construct iterated integral models for this mapping space
through an open-closed homotopy algebra (OCHA) naturally associated to $f$ and
the theory of open-closed Hochschild homology, which may be of independent
interest. Our main theorem states that the resulting map is a quasi-isomorphism
when $M$ is contractible or 2-connected with the rational homotopy type of an
odd sphere, and $N$ is simply connected. This result generalizes Chen's
classical theorem for free loop spaces and, in the above special cases, extends
the theorem of Getzler-Jones for double loop spaces.

</details>

### [3] [Tropical reductive groups and principal bundles on metric graphs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.05422)
*Andreas Gross,Arne Kuhrs,Martin Ulirsch,Dmitry Zakharov*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文提出了热带约化群的基本类比，结合了Weyl群和固定极大环面的热带化。对于经典群和G2，这些热带约化群可以描述为热带矩阵群。作者引入了度量图上的热带主丛，并研究其作为具有对称性和额外数据的覆盖上线丛的推前。主要结果将Tate曲线上半稳定主丛模空间的本质骨架与其热带类比等同起来。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究热带几何与代数几何之间的对应关系，特别是将主丛理论扩展到热带设置，以建立模空间的热带类比。

Method: 提出热带约化群的概念，结合Weyl群和极大环面的热带化；引入度量图上的热带主丛，研究其作为覆盖上线丛推前的表示；通过热带矩阵群描述经典群和G2的热带类比。

Result: 对于经典群和G2，热带约化群可以表示为热带矩阵群；建立了Tate曲线上半稳定主丛模空间的本质骨架与其热带类比之间的等同关系。

Conclusion: 成功构建了热带约化群理论，并将代数几何中的主丛模空间与热带几何中的对应结构联系起来，为两个领域之间的桥梁提供了理论基础。

Abstract: We propose an elementary tropical analogue of a reductive group that combines
the datum of a Weyl group and the tropicalization of a fixed maximal torus. For
the classical groups, as well as $G_2$, these tropical reductive groups admit
descriptions as tropical matrix groups that resemble their classical
counterparts. Employing this perspective, we introduce tropical principal
bundles on metric graphs and study their explicit presentations as pushforwards
of line bundles along covers with symmetries and extra data. Our main result
identifies the essential skeleton of the moduli space of semistable principal
bundles on a Tate curve with its tropical analogue.

</details>

### [6] [Non-homogeneous Koszul duality in representation theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.05140)
*Gwyn Bellamy,Simone Castellan,Isambard Goodbody*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究了关联分次为Koszul的滤过代数U，建立了U的无界导出范畴与其对偶弯曲dg-代数上内射模范畴同伦范畴的显式商之间的精确等价关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受辛反射代数、变形预投射代数和分次Hecke代数的表示理论启发，研究关联分次为Koszul的滤过代数U的导出范畴结构。

Method: 考虑滤过代数U，其关联分次是Koszul代数。利用Positselski定义的Koszul对偶概念，将U的对偶定义为弯曲dg-代数，并建立导出范畴与同伦范畴之间的等价关系。

Result: 证明了U的无界导出范畴与对偶弯曲dg-代数上内射模范畴同伦范畴的显式商之间的精确等价。当U具有有限整体维数时，该商是平凡的，从而得到无界导出范畴与内射模范畴同伦范畴的直接等价。

Conclusion: 本文建立了滤过代数U的导出范畴与其对偶弯曲dg-代数表示范畴之间的深刻联系，推广了Positselski的结果，并为相关代数的表示理论提供了新的视角。

Abstract: Motivated by the representation theory of symplectic reflection algebras,
deformed preprojective algebras, and graded Hecke algebras, we consider
filtered algebras $U$ whose associated graded is Koszul. The Koszul dual of
$U$, as defined by Positselski, is a curved dg-algebra. We establish an exact
equivalence between the unbounded derived category of $U$ and an explicit
quotient of the homotopy category of injective modules over the dual curved
dg-algebra. This recovers a special case of a result of Positselski. In the
case where $U$ has finite global dimension, the quotient is trivial and hence
the unbounded derived category of $U$ is equivalent to the homotopy category of
injective modules over the dual curved dg-algebra.

</details>
