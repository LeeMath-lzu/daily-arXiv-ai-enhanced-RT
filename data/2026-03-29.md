### [1] [Topological K-theory of quasi-BPS categories of symmetric quivers with potential](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.08432)
*Tudor Pădurariu,Yukinobu Toda*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文通过拓扑K理论的循环映射，建立了准BPS范畴与BPS上同调之间的精确联系，证明了准BPS范畴拓扑K理论上存在滤过结构，其分级同构于单值不变的BPS上同调。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 先前的研究引入了准BPS范畴并研究了其在对称箭图、预投射代数等结构中的性质，这些范畴具有类似于枚举几何中BPS不变量/上同调的特性。本文旨在通过拓扑K理论的循环映射，更精确地建立准BPS范畴与BPS上同调之间的联系。

Method: 1. 使用拓扑K理论的循环映射；2. 证明准BPS范畴的拓扑K理论上存在滤过结构；3. 计算矩阵分解范畴的拓扑K理论，用单值不变消失环表示；4. 证明矩阵分解的格罗滕迪克-黎曼-罗赫定理；5. 证明K理论中Koszul等价与上同调中维数约化的相容性。

Result: 1. 准BPS范畴的拓扑K理论上存在滤过结构，其分级同构于单值不变的BPS上同调；2. 矩阵分解范畴的拓扑K理论可以用单值不变消失环表示；3. 建立了矩阵分解的格罗滕迪克-黎曼-罗赫定理；4. 证明了K理论中Koszul等价与上同调中维数约化的相容性。

Conclusion: 本文通过拓扑K理论的循环映射，精确建立了准BPS范畴与BPS上同调之间的联系，为后续研究提供了理论基础。在另一篇论文中，作者将利用这些结果证明K3曲面的准BPS范畴能够恢复相应局部曲面的BPS不变量，即K3曲面上点Hilbert概型的欧拉示性数。

Abstract: In previous works, we introduced and studied certain categories called quasi-BPS categories associated to symmetric quivers with potential, preprojective algebras, and local surfaces. They have properties reminiscent of BPS invariants/ cohomologies in enumerative geometry, for example they play important roles in categorical wall-crossing formulas.
  In this paper, we make the connections between quasi-BPS categories and BPS cohomologies more precise via the cycle map for topological K-theory. We show the existence of filtrations on topological K-theory of quasi-BPS categories whose associated graded are isomorphic to the monodromy invariant BPS cohomologies. Along the way, we also compute the topological K-theory of categories of matrix factorizations in terms of the monodromy invariant vanishing cycles (a version of this comparison was already known by work of Blanc-Robalo-Toën-Vezzosi), prove a Grothendieck-Riemann-Roch theorem for matrix factorizations, and prove the compatibility between the Koszul equivalence in K-theory and dimensional reduction in cohomology.
  In a separate paper, we use the results from this paper to show that the quasi-BPS categories of K3 surfaces recover the BPS invariants of the corresponding local surface, which are Euler characteristics of Hilbert schemes of points on K3 surfaces.

### [2] [A note on gamma factors for pairs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25437)
*Omer Offen*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 该短注指出Gelfand和Kazhdan定义的gamma因子与Jacquet、Piatetskii-Shapiro和Shalika定义的Rankin-Selberg根数是一致的


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在表示论和自守形式理论中，存在两个重要的数学对象：Gelfand-Kazhdan定义的gamma因子和Jacquet-Piatetskii-Shapiro-Shalika定义的Rankin-Selberg根数。作者注意到这两个概念可能描述的是同一数学实体，需要明确它们之间的关系

Method: 通过理论分析和比较两个数学定义，作者观察并证明了这两个概念在数学上的一致性。这是一个理论性的观察，基于对两个定义的深入理解

Result: 作者证明了Gelfand-Kazhdan定义的gamma因子与Jacquet-Piatetskii-Shapiro-Shalika定义的Rankin-Selberg根数实际上是同一个数学对象

Conclusion: 这一观察澄清了表示论和自守形式理论中两个重要概念的关系，表明它们是同一数学实体的不同表述，有助于统一理论框架

Abstract: In this short note we observe that the gamma factor defined by Gelfand and Kazhdan coincides with the Rankin-Selberg root number defined by Jacquet, Piatetskii-Shapiro and Shalika.

</details>
