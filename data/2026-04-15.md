### [11] [The parity operator for parafermions and parabosons](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12393)
*N. I. Stoilova,J. Van der Jeugt*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文通过引入宇称算符P重新审视了parafermions和parabosons的定义，发现其代数结构分别对应正交李代数so(2n+2)和正交超李代数osp(2|2n)。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 重新审视通过格林三重关系定义的parafermions和parabosons，通过引入宇称算符P扩展这些关系，探索新的代数结构。

Method: 使用格林三重关系定义parafermions和parabosons，并引入宇称算符P扩展这些关系，分析相应的代数结构。

Result: n个parafermions与P的代数结构是正交李代数so(2n+2)，其Fock空间对应特定不可约表示；n个parabosons与P的代数结构是正交超李代数osp(2|2n)，其Fock空间对应无限维不可约表示。

Conclusion: 引入宇称算符P后，parafermions和parabosons分别对应经典李代数和超李代数结构，P的谱与格林引入的统计阶数p密切相关。

Abstract: In this paper we reexamine the definition of parafermions and parabosons by means of Green's triple relations, and extend these relations by including a parity operator $P$ which is also determined by means of triple relations. As a consequence, we are dealing with new algebraic structures. It is shown that the algebra underlying a set of $n$ parafermions together with $P$ is the orthogonal Lie algebra $so(2n+2)$. The Fock spaces correspond to particular irreducible representations of $so(2n+2)$, and the action of $P$ in these spaces leads to interesting observations. Next, we show that the algebra underlying a set of $n$ parabosons together with $P$ is the orthosymplectic Lie superalgebra $osp(2|2n)$. In this case, the Fock spaces correspond to certain irreducible infinite-dimensional representations of $osp(2|2n)$. Both for parafermions and parabosons the spectrum of $P$ is closely related to the so-called order of statistics $p$, introduced by Green.

### [12] [A new model for the quantum mechanics of the Hydrogen atom](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14969)
*Joseph Bernstein,Eyal Subag*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种氢原子量子力学系统的新模型，使用四维洛伦兹二次空间中的锥体作为构型空间，在锥体上的L^2函数构成希尔伯特空间，可观测量由锥体上的代数微分算子表示。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 建立氢原子量子力学的新模型，使用锥体作为构型空间以获得更大的几何对称群（O(3,1)），避免使用奇异算子，并通过Schwartz空间自然编码边界条件。

Method: 从四维洛伦兹二次空间(V,q)出发，取相应的锥体C作为构型空间。希尔伯特空间H由锥体上的L^2函数构成，可观测量用锥体上的代数微分算子D(C)表示。引入特殊的Schwartz子空间H^∞作为D(C)-模，薛定谔算子由D(C)中的薛定谔算子族表示。

Result: 在Schwartz空间H^∞中计算薛定谔算子族的谱，证明其与物理中的谱一致，且H^∞中的解对应于物理中的通常解。

Conclusion: 该模型与标准模型的主要区别在于：使用锥体而非ℝ³作为构型空间，几何对称群为O(3,1)而非O(3)⋉ℝ³；仅使用无奇点的代数算子；通过Schwartz空间H^∞自然编码所有边界条件，无需额外施加。

Abstract: In this paper we introduce a new model for the quantum-mechanical system of the hydrogen atom.
  We start with a four-dimensional Lorentzian quadratic space $(V,q)$ and let $C \subset V$ be the corresponding cone.
  The Hilbert space of our model, denoted by $H$, consists of $L^2$ functions on the cone, and observables are represented by operators in the algebra $D(C)$ of algebraic differential operators on $C$. We introduce a distinguished Schwartz subspace $H^{\infty}$ of $H$ that is naturally a $D(C)$-module. The Schrödinger operator in our system is represented by a Schrödinger family of operators in $D(C)$.
  We compute the spectrum of the Schrödinger family in the Schwartz space $H^{\infty}$ and show that it coincides with the spectrum in physics, and that solutions in $H^{\infty}$ correspond to the usual solutions in physics.
  The main differences from the standard model are as follows. First, we use the cone $C$ instead of $\mathbb{R}^3$ as our configuration space. As a result, the group of geometric symmetries of our configuration space is $O(q)\simeq O(3,1)$ rather than $O(3)\ltimes \mathbb{R}^3$. Second, we use only algebraic operators with no singularities. Third, we do not impose any specific boundary conditions on solutions of our equations; these are all encoded in the Schwartz space $H^{\infty}$.

### [13] [On the large genus of Hurwitz numbers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12880)
*Davide Accadia,Danilo Lewański,Giulio Ruzza*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 该论文提出了一个使用基本方法研究Hurwitz理论大亏格渐近行为的通用框架，并将其应用于多种Hurwitz数类型，覆盖了该领域的大部分研究对象。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Hurwitz理论在代数几何、数学物理和组合数学中提供了丰富的计数问题，但需要建立统一框架来研究其大亏格渐近行为。

Method: 开发了一个仅使用基本方法的通用框架，用于研究Hurwitz理论的大亏格渐近行为，可应用于单重、双重、任意固定分歧点数的Hurwitz数，以及包含完整循环、弱单调、严格单调等不同类型分歧的Hurwitz数。

Result: 该框架覆盖了大多数已研究的Hurwitz数类型，包括HCIZ矩阵模型相关函数、Grothendieck儿童绘画、加权Hurwitz数和黎曼球面的Gromov-Witten不变量等，并能恢复Hurwitz、Do-He-Robertson、C. Yang和X. Li等人的已知结果。

Conclusion: 建立了一个统一的基本方法框架，能够系统研究Hurwitz理论的大亏格渐近行为，为这一领域提供了强有力的分析工具。

Abstract: Hurwitz theory provides a large variety of enumerative problems related to algebraic geometry, mathematical physics, and combinatorics. We give a general framework to approach the large genus asymptotics of Hurwitz theory using only elementary methods and apply it to several types of Hurwitz numbers: single, double, or with an arbitrary numbers of fixed ramifications; simple and / or including completed cycles type of ramification and / or finitely many blocks of weakly monotone and / or strictly monotone types of ramifications. These, to the best of our knowledge, cover most of the Hurwitz numbers studied, and include for instance correlators of the HCIZ matrix model, Grothendieck dessins d'enfant, weighted Hurwitz numbers, and Gromov-Witten invariants of the Riemann sphere. We also apply our method to b-content Hurwitz numbers. As a specialisation, we recover some previously known about the large genus asymptotics of Hurwitz theory, namely classical results by Hurwitz and recent results of Do-He-Robertson, C. Yang, and results connected to recent work of X. Li.
