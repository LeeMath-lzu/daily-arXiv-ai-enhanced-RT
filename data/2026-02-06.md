### [1] [Diagonal F-thresholds for determinants and Pfaffians](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05761)
*Barbara Betti,Claudiu Raicu,Francesco Romeo,Jyoti Singh*

Main category: math.AC

TL;DR: 计算了三种矩阵类型（一般矩阵、对称矩阵、偶数阶斜对称矩阵）的行列式超曲面的对角F-阈值，利用特征p下旗簇上线丛的上同调消失定理，发现一般矩阵和斜对称矩阵的对角F-阈值达到最小值（负a-不变量），对称矩阵情况更复杂需要额外多项式性结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究代数几何中F-阈值这一重要不变量，特别是对于由矩阵定义的特殊超曲面（行列式簇和Pfaffian簇），这些簇在代数几何和表示论中具有基础重要性。通过计算这些具体例子的对角F-阈值，可以更好地理解F-阈值的性质和行为。

Method: 主要使用特征p下旗簇上特定线丛的上同调消失定理。对于对称矩阵情况，还需要基于第二作者与VandeBogert工作的上同调表示的多项式性结果。通过代数几何和表示论方法结合，计算三种矩阵类型对应的超曲面的对角F-阈值。

Result: 1. 一般矩阵和偶数阶斜对称矩阵的行列式超曲面对角F-阈值达到其最小可能值，即负a-不变量。2. 对称矩阵情况更为微妙，需要额外的多项式性结果来确定其对角F-阈值。3. 建立了旗簇上线丛的上同调消失定理作为主要技术工具。

Conclusion: 成功计算了三种重要矩阵类型的行列式超曲面的对角F-阈值，揭示了不同矩阵类型下F-阈值行为的差异。一般矩阵和斜对称矩阵达到理论最小值，而对称矩阵需要更精细的分析。这些结果为理解F-阈值在具体几何对象上的表现提供了重要案例，并发展了特征p代数几何中的技术工具。

Abstract: We compute the diagonal F-thresholds of determinantal hypersurfaces arising from a generic matrix and from a generic symmetric matrix, as well as of the Pfaffian hypersurface arising from a generic skew-symmetric matrix of even size. The main ingredient is a cohomology vanishing theorem for certain line bundles on flag varieties in characteristic $p$. In the cases of the generic matrix and the generic skew-symmetric matrix, we show that the diagonal F-threshold attains its minimal possible value, namely the negative of the a-invariant. The symmetric case is more subtle and relies in addition on a polynomiality result for representations afforded by cohomology, building on work of the second author with VandeBogert.

### [2] [Algebraic Geometry for Spin-Adapted Coupled Cluster Theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16646)
*Fabian M. Faulstich,Svala Sverrisdóttir*

Main category: physics.chem-ph

TL;DR: 本文开发了一种基于代数几何的自旋适应耦合簇理论框架，通过识别SU(2)不变（自旋单重态）多体空间与Artinian交换环（激发环）的关系，实现了维度和度数的显著降低，从而大幅减少了计算所有CC解所需的同伦路径数量。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 由于电子哈密顿量具有SU(2)不变性，物理相关的量子态位于自旋单重态扇区。传统自旋广义公式存在维度冗余，需要开发自旋适应的代数几何框架来减少计算复杂度。

Method: 将SU(2)不变（自旋单重态）多体空间识别为Artinian交换环（激发环），其维度由Narayana数控制。通过嵌入该环的分级子空间定义自旋适应截断簇，并将CCS截断簇识别为Grassmannian的Veronese平方。

Result: 与自旋广义公式相比，该方法实现了维度和度数的显著降低，截断簇的CC度数减少了数量级。缩放研究显示了渐近改进，并利用这种降维计算了水和氢化锂的自旋适应CC方程的完整解空间。

Conclusion: 该代数几何框架为自旋适应耦合簇理论提供了数学基础，通过利用SU(2)对称性显著减少了计算复杂度，使得计算完整CC解空间成为可能，为量子化学计算提供了更高效的方法。

Abstract: We develop and numerically analyze an algebraic-geometric framework for spin-adapted coupled-cluster (CC) theory. Since the electronic Hamiltonian is SU(2)-invariant, physically relevant quantum states lie in the spin singlet sector. We give an explicit description of the SU(2)-invariant (spin singlet) many-body space by identifying it with an Artinian commutative ring, called the excitation ring, whose dimension is governed by a Narayana number. We define spin-adapted truncation varieties via embeddings of graded subspaces of this ring, and we identify the CCS truncation variety with the Veronese square of the Grassmannian. Compared to the spin-generalized formulation, this approach yields a substantial reduction in dimension and degree, with direct computational consequences. In particular, the CC degree of the truncation variety -- governing the number of homotopy paths required to compute all CC solutions -- is reduced by orders of magnitude. We present scaling studies demonstrating asymptotic improvements and we exploit this reduction to compute the full solution landscape of spin-adapted CC equations for water and lithium hydride.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Method: 从两个角度进行研究：1) 通过量子簇代数与辫群中辫移动（特别是4-移动和6-移动）产生的同态；2) 通过范畴化理论，特别是与箭袋Hecke代数的联系。

Result: 证明了截断Kirillov-Reshetikhin多项式和标准基元素的系数都在非负多项式环中，为量子Laurent正性猜想提供了有力证据，并推导了非斜对称类型的替换公式。

Conclusion: 量子虚拟格罗滕迪克环中的截断元素具有非负系数性质，这为量子簇代数在非斜对称类型中的Laurent正性猜想提供了重要支持，建立了量子簇代数与范畴化理论之间的深刻联系。

Abstract: In this paper, we study the quantum virtual Grothendieck ring, denoted by $\frakK_q(\g)$, which was introduced in [39], and further investigated in [26, 25]. Our approach involves examining this ring from two perspectives: first, by considering its connection to quantum cluster algebras of non-skew-symmetric types; and second, by exploring its relevance to categorification theory. We specifically focus on (i) the homomorphisms that arise from braid moves, particularly 4-moves and 6-moves, in the braid group; and (ii) the quantum Laurent positivity phenomena, which has not yet been proven for non-skew-symmetric types.
  As applications of our results, we derive the substitution formulas for non-skew-symmetric types discussed in [11] for skew-symmetric types, and demonstrate that any truncated element in a heart subring, denoted by $\frakK_{q,Q}(\g)$, which corresponds to a simple module over the quiver Hecke algebra $R^\g$, possesses coefficients in $\Z_{\ge 0}[q^{\pm 1/2}]$. This result is particularly interesting because it implies that each truncated Kirillov--Reshetikhin polynomial in $\frakK_{q,Q}(\g)$ and each element in the standard basis $\sfE_q(\g)$ of the entire ring $\frakK_q(\g)$ have coefficients also in $\Z_{\ge 0}[q^{\pm 1/2}]$.
  Since (truncated) Kirillov--Reshetikhin polynomials can be obtained using a quantum cluster algebra algorithm and appear as quantum cluster variables, they provide compelling evidence in support of the quantum Laurent positivity conjecture in non-skew-symmetric types.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

### [6] [Estimating the spectral radius of Bell-type operator via finite dimensional approximation of orthogonal projections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.10939)
*Yuki Fujii,Toyohiro Tsurumaru*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 论文建立了希尔伯特空间中两个正交投影P和Q的新分解公式，得到正交直和分解，在每个无限维分量上得到"单移位形式"的矩阵表示，可用于从有限维结果推导无限维结果，并应用于量子力学中贝尔-CHSH算子谱半径的估计。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为分析无限维希尔伯特空间中两个正交投影相关问题提供统一框架，特别是将涉及P和Q的算子谱问题简化为单移位形式，并应用于量子力学中贝尔-CHSH算子谱半径的精确估计。

Method: 建立正交投影P和Q的新分解公式，将希尔伯特空间分解为P和Q的不变子空间直和，每个子空间至多二维或无限维。在无限维分量上构造"单移位形式"的矩阵表示，将两个投影对角化为至多2×2的块，并通过有限维子空间上的正交投影进行显式逼近。

Abstract: We establish a new decomposition formula for two orthogonal projections P and Q on a separable Hilbert space V. This representation diagonalizes both P and Q into blocks of size at most two, and moreover, both projections can be explicitly approximated by orthogonal projections on finite dimensional subspaces. We provide an upper bound and a lower bound for the spectral radius of [P,Q], which become exact when the matrix representations of P and Q are in "constant-angle one-shifted form".
