### [1] [On the Strong Unital Property for the Affine VOAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04187)
*Angela Cai*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了顶点算子代数(VOA)表示中模式转移代数的强幺元性质，特别针对sl2仿射VOA，证明了在非临界水平下模式转移代数不具有强幺元性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 顶点算子代数表示中的模式转移代数如果具有强幺元性质，相关的共形块层会是向量丛而非仅仅是凝聚层。然而强幺元性质在实践中难以验证，需要具体研究特定VOA的强幺元性质。

Method: 针对sl2仿射VOA，构造了L_{\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}_2}}(1,0)的显式强幺元，并证明了对于sl2的通用仿射VOA，在任意非临界水平k≠-2下，模式转移代数都不具有强幺元性质。

Result: 成功构造了L_{\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}_2}}(1,0)的显式强幺元，并证明了对于sl2的通用仿射VOA，当且仅当k=-2（临界水平）时模式转移代数才可能具有强幺元性质，在其他水平下都不具有强幺元性质。

Conclusion: 强幺元性质在顶点算子代数表示理论中具有重要意义，但除了临界水平外，sl2仿射VOA的模式转移代数都不具备这一性质，这为理解VOA表示与几何构造之间的关系提供了重要见解。

Abstract: Representations of vertex operator algebras $V$ (VOAs) have numerous applications, including the construction of sheaves of conformal blocks on moduli spaces of curves. For a $V$-module $W = \oplus W_d$, a sequence of associative algebras $\mathfrak{A}_d$ acts on each graded component $W_d$. When these $d$th-mode transition algebras $\mathfrak{A}_d$ are strongly unital - meaning they are unital with units acting as the identity on $W_d$ - the associated sheaves of conformal blocks are vector bundles rather than merely coherent sheaves. This strong unital property, while difficult to verify in practice, has other important implications as well. Here we construct explicit strong units for $L_{\widehat{\mathfrak{sl}_2}}(1,0)$, the simple affine VOA for $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ at level $1$, and establish that mode transition algebras for universal affine VOAs for $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ are never strongly unital at any level $k$ not equal to the critical level $-2$.

### [2] [The orientifold Temperley--Lieb algebra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04012)

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文将2-边界Temperley-Lieb代数和辛blob代数实现为Varagnolo-Vasserot定向折叠quiver Hecke代数的商，证明了辛blob代数是分级细胞代数，并提出了计算其分级分解矩阵的算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机是为Varagnolo-Vasserot定向折叠quiver Hecke代数提供第一个显式的有限维分级商代数族，借鉴Libedinsky-Plaza的"blob代数方法"来研究模表示论结构。

Method: 通过将辛blob代数实现为定向折叠quiver Hecke代数的商代数，构造了2-边界Temperley-Lieb代数和辛blob代数简单模的分级结构，证明了辛blob代数是分级细胞代数。

Result: 成功构造了相关代数简单模的分级结构，证明了辛blob代数的分级细胞性质，并提出了计算其分级分解矩阵的猜想算法。

Conclusion: 该研究为定向折叠quiver Hecke代数提供了新的研究切入点，建立了与blob代数方法的联系，为模表示论研究开辟了新途径。

Abstract: We construct gradings on the simple modules of 2-boundary Temperley--Lieb algebras and symplectic blob algebras by realising the latter algebras as quotients of Varagnolo--Vasserot's orientifold quiver Hecke algebras. We prove that the symplectic blob algebras are graded cellular and provide a conjectural algorithm for calculating their graded decomposition matrices. In doing so, we give the first explicit family of finite-dimensional graded quotients of the orientifold quiver Hecke algebras, providing a new entry point for the structure of these algebras -- in the spirit of Libedinsky--Plaza's ``blob algebra approach'' to modular representation theory.

### [3] [Affine Hecke and Schur algebras of type A without a square root of q](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04008)
*Rose Berry*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文为A_{n-1}型扩展仿射Hecke代数和仿射q-Schur代数提供了在ℤ[q^{±1}]上的仿射胞腔结构，避免了需要添加q^{1/2}的要求，旨在应用于p-adic群GL_n(F)的表示论。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究动机源于p-adic群GL_n(F)表示论的应用需求，特别是在系数环中p可逆但无平方根的情况下。现有的仿射胞腔结构需要ℤ[q^{±1/2}]，而实际应用中可能需要在ℤ[q^{±1}]上工作，因此需要重新构建胞腔结构。

Method: 通过重新规范化已知在ℤ[q^{±1/2}]上的仿射胞腔结构，在每个左右胞腔上进行调整，确保对角交仍然是子代数且左右胞腔保持同构。同时证明了Schur代数的仿射胞腔结构具有幂等性性质。

Result: 成功构建了在ℤ[q^{±1}]上的仿射胞腔结构，避免了添加q^{1/2}的要求。证明了该结构具有幂等性性质，这暗示了有限全局维度，为p-adic群表示论的应用提供了重要基础。

Conclusion: 该研究为A_{n-1}型扩展仿射Hecke代数和仿射q-Schur代数提供了在ℤ[q^{±1}]上的仿射胞腔结构，解决了实际应用中的技术障碍，为p-adic群GL_n(F)的表示论研究提供了重要工具，特别是系数环中p可逆但无平方根的情况。

Abstract: We provide an affine cellular structure on the extended affine Hecke algebra and affine $q$-Schur algebra of type $A_{n-1}$ that is defined over $\mathbb{Z}\left[q^{\pm1}\right]$, that is, without an adjoined $q^{\frac{1}{2}}$. This is with an eye to applications in the representation theory of $\mathrm{GL}_n(F)$ for a $p$-adic field $F$ over coefficient rings in which $p$ is invertible but does not have a square root, which have been a topic of recent interest. This is achieved via a renormalisation of the known affine cellular structure over $\mathbb{Z}\left[q^{\pm\frac{1}{2}}\right]$ at each left and right cell, which is chosen to ensure that the diagonal intersections remain subalgebras and that the left and right cells remain isomorphic. We furthermore show that the affine cellular structure on the Schur algebra has idempotence properties which imply finite global dimension, an important ingredient for the applications to representations of $p$-adic groups.

Main category: math.RT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究在什么条件下，两个张量范畴的导出范畴之间的等价性可以保证原始模范畴之间的等价性，这对于理解范畴之间的深层结构关系具有重要意义。

Result: 证明了在给定条件下，导出范畴的等价性可以推出原始模范畴的等价性。然后将该结果应用于smash积代数，并研究了模范畴和三角模范畴的局部化理论。

Conclusion: 该论文建立了一个重要的范畴等价性定理：在特定技术条件下，导出范畴之间的等价性可以提升为原始模范畴之间的等价性。这一结果不仅具有理论意义，还可以应用于smash积代数等具体代数结构的研究。

Abstract: Let $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ be subcategories of tensor categories $\mathcal{C}$ and $\mathcal{D}$, respectively, both of which are abelian categories with finitely many isomorphism classes of simple objects. We then apply this result to smash product algebras. Furthermore, the localization theory of module categories and triangulated module categories is investigated.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 对于只有有限多个单对象同构类的阿贝尔长度范畴，存在由半稳定子范畴和半稳定挠对定义的wall-chamber结构和TF等价。本文旨在引入M-TF等价作为系统粗化TF等价的方法，以更好地理解这些结构。

Method: 引入M-TF等价关系，研究其性质，证明M-TF等价类闭包的集合构成有限完全扇，并建立该扇与牛顿多面体正规扇的关系。

Result: 证明了对于每个对象M，M-TF等价类闭包的集合Σ(M)构成K₀(A)ℝ*中的有限完全扇，且该扇是牛顿多面体N(M)在K₀(A)ℝ中的正规扇。

In this paper, we introduce the $M$-TF equivalence for each object $M \in \mathcal{A}$ as a systematic way to coarsen the TF equivalence. We show that the set $Σ(M)$ of closures of $M$-TF equivalence classes is a finite complete fan in $K_0(\mathcal{A})_\mathbb{R}^*$, and that $Σ(M)$ is the normal fan of the Newton polytope $\mathrm{N}(M)$ in $K_0(\mathcal{A})_\mathbb{R}$.
