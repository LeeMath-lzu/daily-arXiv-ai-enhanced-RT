### [1] [On the Quantum K-theory of Quiver Varieties at Roots of Unity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.19383)
*Peter Koroteev,Andrey Smirnov*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文证明 Nakajima 簇的量子差分解的基本解矩阵在 p 次单位根处无极点，并由此给出了量子连接 p-曲率谱的显式描述。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究 Nakajima 簇量子差分解在 p 次单位根处的极点行为，进而理解有限特征域上量子微分方程的 p-曲率谱。

Method: 通过证明 Ψ(z,a,q)Ψ(z^p,a^p,q^{p^2})⁻¹ 在 q=ζ_p 处无极点，分析迭代算子乘积与 Frobenius 扭转的关系，利用 Grothendiek-Katz p-曲率理论建立联系。

Result: 证明了基本解矩阵在 p 次单位根处无极点；迭代算子乘积在 q=ζ_p 处的特征值与 M_L(z^p,a^p,q^p) 相同；给出了量子连接 p-曲率谱的显式描述。

Conclusion: 该结果建立了量子差分解与有限特征域量子微分方程 p-曲率之间的桥梁，为 Nakajima 簇的量子连接提供了具体的谱信息。

Abstract: Let $Ψ(\textbf{z},\textbf{a},q)$ a the fundamental solution matrix of the quantum difference equation of a Nakajima variety $X$. In this work, we prove that the operator $$ Ψ(\textbf{z},\textbf{a},q) Ψ\left(\textbf{z}^p,\textbf{a}^p,q^{p^2}\right)^{-1} $$ has no poles at the primitive complex $p$-th roots of unity $q=ζ_p$. As a byproduct, we show that the iterated product of the operators ${\bf M}_{\mathcal{L}}(\textbf{z},\textbf{a},q )$ from the $q$-difference equation on $X$: $$ {\bf M}_{\mathcal{L}} (\textbf{z} q^{(p-1)\mathcal{L}},\textbf{a},q) \cdots {\bf M}_{\mathcal{L}} (\textbf{z} q^{\mathcal{L}},\textbf{a},q) {\bf M}_{\mathcal{L}} (\textbf{z} ,\textbf{a},q) $$ evaluated at $q=ζ_p$ has the same eigenvalues as ${\bf M}_{\mathcal{L}} (\textbf{z}^p,\textbf{a}^p,q^p)$.
  Upon a reduction of the quantum difference equation of $X$ to the quantum differential equation over the field of finite characteristic, the above iterated product transforms into a Grothendiek-Katz $p$-curvature of the corresponding quantum connection whreas ${\bf M}_{\mathcal{L}} (\textbf{z}^p,\textbf{a}^p,q^p)$ becomes a certain Frobenius twist of that connection. In this way, we give an explicit description of the spectrum of the $p$-curvature of quantum connection for Nakajima varieties.

### [2] [Non-commutative crepant resolutions of toric singularities with divisor class group of rank one](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26252)
*Ryu Tomonaga*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文证明了秩一除子类群的Gorenstein环面奇点存在环面非交换预解（NCCR）并给出了分类，证明了所有此类NCCR可通过迭代突变相连，在非金字塔情形给出了二聚体模型实现，并在附录中给出了d+2个顶点的d维格点多面体的体积公式，验证了Van den Bergh猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究Gorenstein环面奇点的非交换预解（NCCR）的存在性与分类，并为Van den Bergh关于NCCR不可分解直和项数目与正规化体积相等的猜想提供验证。

Method: 利用除子类群的上集（upper set）与NCCR一一对应进行分类；通过Iyama–Wemyss突变证明所有NCCR之间可由迭代突变连接；在非金字塔情形，在d维环面上构造周期割元环（periodic quivers with cuts），建立割与上集的对应关系，并证明所得割元环给出了对应的NCCR。附录给出d+2个顶点的d维格点多面体体积的显式公式。

Result: 得到了秩一除子类群Gorenstein环面奇点的环面NCCR的完全分类；证明了所有此类NCCR通过迭代Iyama–Wemyss突变相互连接，因而导出等价；在非金字塔情形给出了二聚体模型实现；验证了Van den Bergh猜想。

Conclusion: 环面NCCR的分类与上集一一对应，所有NCCR导出等价，且二聚体模型可作为非金字塔情形的统一构造框架，同时Van den Bergh猜想在秩一情况下成立。

Abstract: We prove the existence and give a classification of toric non-commutative crepant resolutions (NCCRs) of Gorenstein toric singularities whose divisor class group has rank one. More precisely, such toric NCCRs are in bijection with non-trivial upper sets in a certain quotient of the divisor class group equipped with a natural partial order. This classification allows us to prove that all toric NCCRs of such toric singularities are connected by iterated Iyama--Wemyss mutations, and hence are derived equivalent to one another.
  We further give a dimer-model realization of this classification in the non-pyramidal case. More precisely, we construct periodic quivers with cuts on a $d$-dimensional torus, establish a cut-upper set correspondence, and prove that the resulting cut quiver with relations presents the corresponding toric NCCR. For $d=2$, this recovers the quiver-theoretic part of the usual dimer-model construction.
  In the appendix, we give an explicit formula for the volume of $d$-dimensional lattice polytopes with $d+2$ vertices. As an application, we verify Van den Bergh's conjectural equality, for Gorenstein toric singularities with divisor class group of rank one, between the number of indecomposable direct summands of a toric NCCR and the normalized volume of the corresponding lattice polytope.

### [3] [Local Representations of the Flat Virtual Braid Group](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.06607)
*Mohamad N. Nasser,Mohammad Y. Chreif,Malak M. Dally*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文分类了平坦虚拟辫群 $FVB_n$ 到 $GL_m(\mathbb{C})$ 的复局部表示，给出了可约性、不可约性和忠信性的充要条件或充分条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 旨在系统分类和理解平坦虚拟辫群的局部表示结构，特别是其在低维一般线性群中的表示类型、可约性与忠信性。

Method: 通过代数分类方法，对 $FVB_2$ 到 $GL_2(\mathbb{C})$、$FVB_n$ 到 $GL_n(\mathbb{C})$ 和 $GL_{n+1}(\mathbb{C})$ 的复局部表示进行类型划分，并利用参数条件分析其可约性和忠信性。

Result: 得到了 $FVB_2$ 的12种表示类型（其中前5类可约性条件给出，后7类可约）；$FVB_n$ 到 $GL_n$ 的2种类型（$\gamma_1$ 在 $n\ge6$ 时可约，$\gamma_2$ 在 $b\neq y$ 时不可约）；$FVB_n$ 到 $GL_{n+1}$ 的8种类型（$n\ge10$ 时可约）。忠信性方面给出了各类型的充要或充分条件。

Conclusion: 完整刻画了平坦虚拟辫群在低维复一般线性群中的局部表示分类，为后续研究提供了理论基础。

Abstract: We prove that any complex local representation of the flat virtual braid group, $FVB_2$, into $GL_2(\mathbb{C})$, has one of the types $λ_i: FVB_2 \rightarrow GL_2(\mathbb{C})$, $1\leq i\leq 12$. We find necessary and sufficient conditions that guarantee the irreducibility of representations of type $λ_i$, $1\leq i\leq 5$, and we prove that representations of type $λ_i$, $6\leq i\leq 12$, are reducible. Regarding faithfulness, we find necessary and sufficient conditions for representations of type $λ_6$ or $λ_7$ to be faithful. Moreover, we give sufficient conditions for representations of type $λ_1$, $λ_2$, or $λ_4$ to be unfaithful, and we show that representations of type $λ_i$, $i=3, 5, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12$ are unfaithful. We prove that any complex homogeneous local representations of the flat virtual braid group, $FVB_n$, into $GL_{n}(\mathbb{C})$, for $n\geq 3$, has one of the types $γ_i: FVB_n \rightarrow GL_n(\mathbb{C})$, $i=1, 2$. We then prove that representations of type $γ_1: FVB_n \rightarrow GL_n(\mathbb{C})$ are reducible for $n\geq 6$, while representations of type $γ_2: FVB_n \rightarrow GL_n(\mathbb{C})$ are irreducible if and only if $b\neq y$, for $n\geq 3$. Then, we show that representations of type $γ_1$ are unfaithful for $n\geq 3$ and that representations of type $γ_2$ are unfaithful if $y=b$. Furthermore, we prove that any complex homogeneous local representation of the flat virtual braid group, $FVB_n$, into $GL_{n+1}(\mathbb{C})$, for all $n\geq 4$, has one of the types $δ_i: FVB_n \rightarrow GL_{n+1}(\mathbb{C})$, $1\leq i\leq 8$. We prove that these representations are reducible for $n\geq 10$. Then, we show that representations of types $δ_i$, $i\neq 5, 6$, are unfaithful, while representations of types $δ_5$ or $δ_6$ are unfaithful if $x=y$.

</details>


### [4] [Bubble sort and Howe duality for staircase matrices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.21184)
*Anton Khoroshkin,Ievgen Makedonskyi*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文基于Bruhat偏序集和冒泡排序的组合学，为楼梯矩阵的Cauchy恒等式提供了独立证明，并推导了其中某些系数的新见解。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 对arXiv:2411.03117中发现的楼梯矩阵Cauchy恒等式给出一个独立的组合证明，并深入理解其中出现的系数。

Method: 利用Bruhat偏序集与冒泡排序的组合学；引入树状偏序集上单调函数的抛物型Bruhat图的推广；研究可积表示中Demazure模的交集；通过Demazure子模的分配格推广经典Howe对偶性。

Result: 得到了楼梯矩阵Cauchy恒等式的独立组合证明，推导了恒等式中某些系数的新性质，并提出了基于Demazure子模分配格的Howe对偶性推广。

Conclusion: 通过组合与表示论两个视角，为楼梯矩阵的Cauchy恒等式提供了新的证明方法和理论推广。

Abstract: In this paper, we present an independent proof of the Cauchy identities for staircase matrices, originally discovered in arXiv:2411.03117, using the combinatorics of the Bruhat poset and the bubble-sort procedure. Additionally, we derive new insights into certain coefficients appearing in one of these identities.
  The first part of the paper focuses on combinatorial aspects. It is self-contained, of independent interest, and introduces a generalization of parabolic Bruhat graphs for monotone functions on an arborescent poset. The second part examines the intersections of Demazure modules within a given integrable representation. Finally, we propose a generalization of the classical Howe duality for staircase matrices in terms of the corresponding distributive lattice of Demazure submodules. Computing the associated character yields the desired Cauchy identities for staircase matrices.

</details>
