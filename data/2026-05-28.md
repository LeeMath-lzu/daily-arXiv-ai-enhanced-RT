### [1] [From geodesic flow to wave dynamics on hyperbolic surfaces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28243)
*Frédéric Faure*

Main category: math.SP

TL;DR: 本文通过构造显式的X-适应Hilbert空间，将双曲曲面的测地流转化为正规算子（除阈值1/4处可能出现Jordan块），并揭示了传播子分解为阻尼谐振子与移位的波动算子。该模型阐明了Selberg迹公式的动力学形式以及球平均算子的长时间行为。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 利用SL₂(ℝ)表示论，建立双曲曲面测地流（生成元X）的显式谱理论与Hilbert空间结构，从而统一测地动力学、Ruelle共振与曲面谱理论之间的联系。

Method: 1) 构造X-适应Hilbert空间（传播稠密集的完备化）；2) 利用SL₂(ℝ)表示论将传播子e^{tX}分解为阻尼谐振子部分与横向部分（移位的波动群±it√(Δ-1/4)及离散序列表示）；3) 比较该模型中的谱迹与Atiyah-Bott-Guillemin平坦迹。

Result: 1) X在适应空间中成为离散谱正规算子（μ=1/4处有Jordan块）；2) 传播子的谱迹与平坦迹比较导出了Selberg迹公式的动力学形式；3) 经e^{t/2}重正规化并移除低能部分后，球平均算子的有效动力学由移位的波动方程主导。

Conclusion: 该Hilbert空间模型显式地统一了几何测地流、Ruelle共振与曲面谱理论，为理解双曲动力学与谱几何的深层联系提供了清晰的算子理论框架。

Abstract: We study the geodesic flow on the unit cotangent bundle $M=S^{*}\mathcal{N}$ of a closed hyperbolic surface $\mathcal{N}$, using the representation theory of $SL_{2}(\mathbb{R})$. We construct explicit $X$-adapted Hilbert spaces, obtained by completing propagated dense domains of $L^{2}(M)$, which are tailored to the spectral analysis of the geodesic generator $X$. In these spaces, $X$ becomes a normal operator with discrete spectrum, except at the threshold $μ=1/4$, where Jordan blocks of size two may occur.
  In this Hilbert model, the propagator $e^{tX}$ factorizes into a damped harmonic oscillator with eigenvalues $e^{-t(n+1/2)}$, $n\in\mathbb{N}$, and a transverse part involving the shifted wave group $e^{\pm it\sqrt{Δ-1/4}}$ on $\mathcal{N}$, together with the holomorphic and anti-holomorphic discrete series.
  The model clarifies two classical links between geodesic dynamics and the Laplace spectrum. Comparing the spectral trace of the propagator in the $X$-adapted Hilbert model with the Atiyah--Bott--Guillemin flat trace gives a dynamical form of the Selberg trace formula: closed geodesics arise from the flat trace, while the spectral side comes from the explicit $SL_{2}(\mathbb{R})$-decomposition. The same factorization also explains the large-time structure of spherical mean operators on $\mathcal{N}$: after the natural $e^{t/2}$-renormalization and the removal of a finite-rank low-energy part, the shifted wave equation on $\mathcal{N}$ emerges as the leading effective dynamics. Thus the construction provides an explicit Hilbert-space structure relating classical geodesic dynamics, Ruelle resonances, and the spectral theory of the surface.

Method: 通过发展沿简单正规交叉除数的多变量V-滤过理论，建立其与Sabbah多滤过的对应关系，并利用几何表示论中的自由单子局部系统的Hodge滤过建立结构结果。

Conclusion: 本文建立的多变量V-滤过理论为相关问题提供了统一且概念化的处理框架，有效解决了多个重要猜想。

Abstract: In this work, we develop a new theory of multivariate V-filtration on D-modules along a simple normal crossing divisor and relate it with Sabbah's multi-filtration. We establish several new structural results and relate them with the Hodge filtration on free-monodromic local systems from geometric representation theory. As an illustrative application, we give a conceptual and very quick proof of the Strong Monodromy Conjecture and its multivariate generalisation for hyperplane arrangements. Along the way, we confirm both the n/d-conjecture of Budur--Mustaţă--Teitler and its multivariate form due to Budur.

### [3] [Higher representation infinite algebras and toric Fano stacks of Picard number one or two](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02641)
*Ryu Tomonaga*

Main category: math.AG

TL;DR: 本文研究了光滑射影簇上 $d$-tilting 丛的存在性与分类问题，分别对 Picard 数为一和二的光滑环法诺栈，证明了由线丛构成的 $d$-tilting 丛与某种上集之间的双射关系，并将其自同态代数分别刻画为 $\widetilde A$ 型和 $\widetilde A\widetilde A$ 型的高阶表示无限代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: $d$-tilting 丛在几何与表示理论之间搭建桥梁，尤其是与导出McKay对应和高阶Auslander-Reiten理论相关。本文旨在证明并分类特定维数光滑环法诺栈上的 $d$-tilting 丛。

Method: 利用Picard群上的偏序结构，建立非平凡上集与 $d$-tilting 丛之间的双射；通过引入高阶表示无限代数 $\widetilde A$ 和 $\widetilde A\widetilde A$ 类型，刻画自同态代数；并利用几何模型给出 $d$-APR tilting 模的组合描述。

Result: Picard数一时，线丛tilting丛与非平凡上集一一对应，所有均为$d$-tilting丛，其自同态代数为$\widetilde A$型高阶表示无限代数。Picard数二时，$d$-tilting丛与上集对$(I,I')$一一对应，其自同态代数为$\widetilde A\widetilde A$型高阶表示无限代数。

Conclusion: 光滑环法诺栈可作为高阶表示无限代数的几何模型，并证明了这些代数类在 $d$-APR tilt下封闭。

Abstract: On smooth projective varieties of dimension $d$, $d$-tilting bundles are important in both geometry and representation theory, since they provide a bridge from the geometry of such varieties to the derived McKay correspondence and to higher Auslander--Reiten theory. Here, a $d$-tilting bundle means a tilting bundle whose endomorphism algebra has global dimension at most $d$. In this paper, we prove the existence of and classify all $d$-tilting bundles consisting of line bundles on $d$-dimensional smooth toric Fano stacks of Picard number one or two.
  In the case of Picard number one, tilting bundles consisting of line bundles are in bijection with non-trivial upper sets in its Picard group equipped with a certain partial order. Moreover, all of them are $d$-tilting bundles and their endomorphism algebras are $d$-representation infinite algebras of type $\widetilde A$. Conversely, all such algebras arise in this way.
  In the case of Picard number two, $d$-tilting bundles consisting of line bundles are in bijection with pairs $(I,I')$, where $I$ and $I'$ are non-trivial upper sets in certain partially ordered sets. Here, $I$ corresponds to a non-commutative crepant resolution (NCCR) of a certain toric singularity and $I'$ corresponds to a cut of the quiver of this NCCR. We introduce higher representation infinite algebras of type $\widetilde A\widetilde A$ and show that the endomorphism algebras of these $d$-tilting bundles belong to this class. Conversely, all such algebras arise in this way.
  Thus smooth toric Fano stacks of Picard number one (respectively, two) can be regarded as geometric models of higher representation infinite algebras of type $\widetilde A$ (respectively, $\widetilde A\widetilde A$). Using these geometric models, we show that these classes of algebras are closed under $d$-APR tilts by giving a combinatorial description of their $d$-APR tilting modules.

### [4] [A categorification of combinatorial Auslander-Reiten quivers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.06147)
*Ricardo Canesin*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文在2维Ginzburg dg代数的完美值导出范畴中，对Oh和Suh的组合Auslander-Reiten箭图进行了范畴化，定义了与Weyl群约化词交换类相关的子范畴，推广了Bédard的结果，并构造了类似有界导出范畴的商范畴，建立了网格可加性，定义了欧拉形式并证明其对称化得到Cartan-Killing形式，最后应用于量子Cartan矩阵逆的公式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 对Oh和Suh的组合Auslander-Reiten箭图在 simply laced 情形下进行范畴化，推广Bédard的结果，并建立与导出范畴、量子群等相关理论的联系。

Method: 在2维Ginzburg dg代数的完美值导出范畴pvd(Π_Q)中，对每个约化词的交换类[i]定义子范畴C([i])，通过球面扭转函子作用于简单对象得到；进而构造子商范畴D([i])；证明特定 distinguished triangle 的存在性；定义欧拉形式并证明其对称性。

Result: 1. 描述了C([i])的Hom-order与交换类[i]的关系，推广了Bédard的结果；2. 当[i]为最长元的交换类时，构造了D([i])推广了Q的有界导出范畴；3. 证明了网格可加性对任意交换类成立；4. 欧拉形式的对称化得到Cartan-Killing形式；5. 对Q-data情形，证明了D([i])上存在部分Serre函子；6. 重新解释了Fujita和Oh的量子Cartan矩阵逆公式。

Conclusion: 本文成功将组合Auslander-Reiten箭图范畴化，并建立了与导出范畴、量子群理论的深刻联系，推广了多个经典结果。

Abstract: We provide a categorification of Oh and Suh's combinatorial Auslander-Reiten quivers in the simply laced case. We work within the perfectly valued derived category $\mathrm{pvd}(Π_Q)$ of the 2-dimensional Ginzburg dg algebra of a Dynkin quiver $Q$. For any commutation class $[i]$ of reduced words in the corresponding Weyl group, we define a subcategory $C([i])$ of $\mathrm{pvd}(Π_Q)$ whose objects are obtained by applying a sequence of spherical twist functors to the simple objects. We describe the Hom-order for $C([i])$ in terms of $[i]$, generalizing a result of Bédard. Furthermore, when $[i]$ is a commutation class for the longest element, we construct a category $D([i])$ generalizing the bounded derived category of $Q$. It is realized as a certain subquotient of $\mathrm{pvd}(Π_Q)$. We demonstrate the existence of particular distinguished triangles in $\mathrm{pvd}(Π_Q)$ with corners in $D([i])$, which allows us to extend the classical mesh-additivity to arbitrary commutation classes. Additionally, we define an analog of the Euler form and prove that its symmetrization yields the corresponding Cartan-Killing form. For commutation classes $[i]$ arising from Q-data, a generalization of Dynkin quivers with a height function introduced by Fujita and Oh, we establish the existence of a partial Serre functor on $D([i])$. Lastly, we apply our results to reinterpret a formula by Fujita and Oh for the inverse of the quantum Cartan matrix.
