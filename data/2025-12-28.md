### [1] [Quantum Universality in Composite Systems: A Trichotomy of Clifford Resources](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.20787)
*Alejandro Borda,Julian Rincon,César Galindo*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文研究了高维量子系统（qudit）中通用量子计算的资源需求，发现希尔伯特空间维度d的数论结构严格决定了打破Clifford电路经典模拟屏障所需的资源。对于互质维度架构，仅使用经典纠缠操作（广义CNOT门）即可实现通用计算，无需显式注入"魔法"资源。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Clifford电路的高效经典模拟是量子优势的基本障碍，通常需要注入非Clifford"魔法"资源来克服。本文旨在研究高维量子系统中，希尔伯特空间维度d的数论结构如何严格决定打破这一屏障所需的资源。

Method: 通过分析Clifford群的伴随作用，建立了单qudit通用性的三分法分类。证明依赖于一个新的几何准则：如果一个子群具有不可约的伴随作用，并且包含一个与恒等元投影距离严格小于1/2的非标量元素，则该子群是无限的。

Result: 1. 对于素数维度，Clifford群是最大有限子群，任何非Clifford门都能稳健实现通用性；2. 对于素数幂维度，群结构分裂，需要定制的对角非Clifford门来恢复不可约性；3. 对于具有互质因子的复合维度，仅使用广义intra-qudit CNOT门等标准纠缠操作就能生成必要的非Clifford资源，无需显式对角魔法注入。

Conclusion: "互质架构"——结合具有互质维度的子系统的混合寄存器——可以仅使用经典纠缠操作来维持通用计算，使得显式注入魔法资源在代数上变得不必要。这为量子计算架构设计提供了新的理论指导。

Abstract: The efficient classical simulation of Clifford circuits constitutes a fundamental barrier to quantum advantage, typically overcome by injecting explicit non-Clifford "magic" resources. We demonstrate that for high-dimensional quantum systems (qudits), the resources required to break this barrier are strictly governed by the number-theoretic structure of the Hilbert space dimension $d$. By analyzing the adjoint action of the Clifford group, we establish a classification of single-qudit universality as a trichotomy. (I) For prime dimensions, the Clifford group is a maximal finite subgroup, and universality is robustly achieved by any non-Clifford gate. (II) For prime-power dimensions, the group structure fragments, requiring tailored diagonal non-Clifford gates to restore irreducibility. (III) Most notably, for composite dimensions with coprime factors, we demonstrate that standard entangling operations alone -- specifically, generalized intra-qudit CNOT gates -- generate the necessary non-Clifford resources to guarantee a dense subgroup of $\mathrm{SU}(d)$ without explicit diagonal magic injection. Our proofs rely on a new geometric criterion establishing that a subgroup with irreducible adjoint action is infinite if it contains a non-scalar element with projective distance strictly less than $1/2$ from the identity. These results establish that "coprime architectures" -- hybrid registers combining subsystems with coprime dimensions -- can sustain universal computation using only classical entangling operations, rendering the explicit injection of magic resources algebraically unnecessary.

### [2] [Relation between generalized and ordinary cluster algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21062)
*Ryota Akagi,Tomoki Nakanishi*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文证明了任意带有y变量的广义丛代数在任意半域中都具有与几何型广义丛代数相同的性质，即同构于某个丛代数的子代数的商代数。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Ramos和Whiting先前证明了几何型广义丛代数同构于某个丛代数的子代数的商代数。本研究旨在将这一结果推广到更一般的广义丛代数，即包含y变量且在任意半域中的广义丛代数。

Method: 基于Ramos和Whiting的思想和方法，将他们的证明技术扩展到任意半域中的广义丛代数。同时研究了广义丛模式与对应复合丛模式之间的C矩阵、G矩阵和F多项式的关系。

Result: 证明了任意带有y变量的广义丛代数在任意半域中都同构于某个丛代数的子代数的商代数。建立了广义丛模式与复合丛模式之间C矩阵、G矩阵和F多项式的具体关系。

Conclusion: 成功将Ramos和Whiting关于几何型广义丛代数的结果推广到更一般的广义丛代数，为广义丛代数的结构研究提供了更完整的理论框架。

Abstract: Recently, Ramos and Whiting showed that any generalized cluster algebra of geometric type is isomorphic to a quotient of a subalgebra of a certain cluster algebra. Based on their idea and method, we show that the same property holds for any generalized cluster algebra with $y$-variables in an arbitrary semifield. We also present the relations between the $C$-matrices, the $G$-matrices, and the $F$-polynomials of a generalized cluster pattern and those of the corresponding composite cluster pattern.
