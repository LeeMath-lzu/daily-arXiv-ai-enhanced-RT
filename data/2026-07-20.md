### [1] [Wide subcategories and brick-finiteness for length categories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15991)
*Francesco Sentieri*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文将有限维代数中 brick-finiteness 的表征推广到阿贝尔长度范畴（有限秩），证明该范畴是 brick-finite 当且仅当每个 torsion 类由宽子范畴生成且每个 torsionfree 类由宽子范畴余生成，并据此将第一 Brick Brauer-Thrall 猜想推广到该设定。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将已知的有限维代数中 brick-finiteness 的表征推广到更一般的阿贝尔长度范畴（有限秩）中。

Method: 基于宽区间上 brick 标签的交换关系，该关系是简单心集合突变的影子，并利用 torsion 类与宽子范畴之间的生成/余生成关系进行证明。

Result: 证明了阿贝尔长度范畴（有限秩）是 brick-finite 当且仅当每个 torsion 类由宽子范畴生成且每个 torsionfree 类由宽子范畴余生成。

Conclusion: 将第一 Brick Brauer-Thrall 猜想的有效性扩展到了阿贝尔长度范畴（有限秩）这一更广泛的设定中。

Abstract: We extend to abelian length categories of finite rank a characterisation of brick-finiteness known for finite-dimensional algebras. We prove that such a category is brick-finite if and only if every torsion class is generated by a wide subcategory and every torsionfree class is cogenerated by a wide subcategory. The proof is based on an exchange relation for brick labels across wide intervals which is a shadow of the mutation of simple minded collections. As a corollary,we extend the validity of the first Brick Brauer-Thrall conjecture to this setting.

### [3] [Lusztig's special pieces conjecture](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15406)
*Daniel Juteau,Paul Levy,Eric Sommers,Shilin Yu*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文证明了特殊幂零轨道上的特殊片P(O)是光滑G-簇X在有限群H作用下的商空间，给出了两种证明方法，并探讨了H与Weyl群族及Springer对应的关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: Lusztig猜想在例外群中成立，且Kraft和Procesi已在经典Lie代数中证明该结果，本文旨在给出统一证明并探讨H的嵌入问题。

Method: 第一种方法利用横截片的已有结果推导；第二种方法将X构造为g与若干基本权重表示直和中的G-轨道闭包的子簇。

Result: 两种方法均证明了特殊片是光滑G-簇对有限群H的商；经典情形给出了新证明；例外群验证了Lusztig猜想。

Conclusion: 存在多个满足条件的G-簇X，它们与H在O的基本群中的自然嵌入相关；附录中阐明了该嵌入与Lusztig通过Weyl群族和Springer对应定义的H之间的关系。

Abstract: Let $\mathcal O$ be a special nilpotent orbit in the Lie algebra $\mathfrak g$ of a simple algebraic group $G$. We give two proofs of the result that every special piece ${\mathcal P}(\mathcal O)$ in $\mathfrak g$ is the quotient of a smooth $G$-variety $X$ by the action of a certain finite group $H$. We first deduce the result from a similar result for transverse slices, established in earlier work of the first three authors and Fu. Then we give a more explicit construction of $X$, as a subvariety of the closure of a $G$-orbit in the direct sum of $\mathfrak g$ and some fundamental weight representations of $G$. Both methods apply to classical $\mathfrak g$, where we give new proofs of this result, which was first proved by Kraft and Procesi. The result in the exceptional groups was conjectured by Lusztig. Our first proof shows that there can be several $G$-varieties $X$ that satisfy the conjecture, related to a natural embedding of $H$ in the fundamental group of $\mathcal O$. In an appendix, we relate this natural embedding to Lusztig's definition of $H$ that arises from the family in the Weyl group of $G$ attached to $\mathcal O$ and from the Springer correspondence.

### [5] [Exact structures and maximal canonically Jordan recoverable subcategories for modules over type $A$ algebras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.25012)
*Benjamin Dequêne,Sunny Roy*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文通过引入由精确结构参数化的Gen-Sub算子，建立了遗传阿贝尔范畴中精确结构与Jordan可恢复性之间的联系，特别在A型箭图路代数上证明了极大典范Jordan可恢复子范畴恰好是由某个倾斜对象生成的Gen-Sub范畴。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 连接D. Quillen在70年代提出的精确结构理论与A. Garver等人近期提出的Jordan可恢复性概念，揭示两者之间的深层联系。

Method: 引入由精确结构参数化的Gen-Sub算子，在遗传阿贝尔范畴中研究其性质，并聚焦于带有钻石精确结构的A型箭图路代数模范畴。

Result: 建立了在A型箭图路代数上，极大典范Jordan可恢复子范畴恰好是形如GS_◇(T)的Gen-Sub算子作用结果，其中T为某个倾斜对象。

Conclusion: 精确结构与Jordan可恢复性之间存在深刻联系，Gen-Sub算子为理解这种联系提供了统一框架。

Abstract: On one hand, exact structures were introduced by D. Quillen in the '70s. They can be defined as collections of short exact sequences in a fixed abelian category satisfying additional properties.
  On the other hand, in a recent work, A. Garver, R. Patrias, and H. Thomas introduced Jordan recoverability. Given a bounded quiver $(Q,R)$, a full additive subcategory of $\operatorname{rep}(Q,R)$ is said to be Jordan recoverable if any $X \in \mathscr{C}$ can be recovered, up to isomorphism, from the Jordan form of its generic nilpotent endomorphisms. Such a subcategory $\mathscr{C}$ is said to be canonically Jordan recoverable if, moreover, there exists a precise algebraic procedure that allows one to get back $X \in \mathscr{C}$ from that same Jordan form data.
  We introduce a new family of operators, called Gen-Sub operators $\operatorname{GS}_\mathcal{E}$, parametrized by the exact structures $\mathcal{E}$ of abelian categories. After showing some properties of those operators in hereditary abelian categories, by focusing on the setting of modules over path algebras of type $A$ quivers endowed with the diamond exact structure $\mathcal{E}_\diamond$, we establish that the maximal canonically Jordan recoverable subcategories are precisely of the form $\operatorname{GS}_{\mathcal{E}_\diamond}(T)$ for some tilting objects $T$.

*Atsushi Nakayashiki*

Main category: nlin.SI

TL;DR: 本文研究了KP层次中tau函数的加法公式，通过极限过程将2n参数移位公式简化为2参数形式的Wronskian行列式，并应用于证明顶点算子和Darboux变换等价，以及推导黎曼theta函数的新加法公式。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: KP层次中tau函数的加法公式已知，但需要进一步探索其极限形式以及与非移位的Wronskian表达式的联系，并应用于证明顶点算子与Darboux变换的等价性，以及推导黎曼theta函数的新加法公式。

Method: 从KP层次tau函数的多参数移位加法公式出发，通过将部分参数取极限（趋于零），得到以Wronskian形式表达的移位tau函数公式，进而分析其特殊情形和应用。

Result: 成功建立了移位tau函数与波函数谱变量代入所得函数的Wronskian之间的联系，证明了顶点算子与Darboux变换的等价性，并推导了黎曼theta函数的一个新加法公式（作为Fay公式的另一种极限）。

Abstract: It is well known that tau functions of the KP hierarchy satisfy addition formulas. Among them we consider the formula which expresses a tau function with shifted arguments by $2n$ parameters in terms of the same tau function with shifted arguments by two parameters in the form of determinant. We then take the limits of it tending some of parameters to zero. As an important special case we obtain the formula which connects the shifted tau function to the Wronskian of functions obtained by substituting parameter values into the spectral variable of the wave function. As an application, we prove the equivalence of vertex operators and Darboux transformations. As another application, we derive a new addition formula for Riemann's theta functions of Riemann surfaces by considering theta function solutions of the KP hierarchy. It can be considered as a limit of Fay's famous formula (43) in the book [Lecture Notes in Math., Vol. 352, Springer, Berlin, 1973]. But the formula we have derived is a different limit from the limits of (43) considered in that book.

### [8] [On some Grothendieck expansions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.18963)
*Eric Marberg,Jiayi Wen*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文研究正交群作用在完全旗簇上的轨道闭包对应的K-理论类（正交Grothendieck多项式），特别关注当索引z为vexillary时，其向普通Grothendieck多项式的展开公式及稳定性性质。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 正交Grothendieck多项式比辛Grothendieck多项式更难处理，其向普通Grothendieck多项式的展开系数大多未知，需要建立新的计算方法。

Method: 针对vexillary情形推导正交Grothendieck多项式的新公式及其Grothendieck展开表达式。

Result: 得到了vexillary正交Grothendieck多项式的若干新公式，证明其Grothendieck展开具有非平凡稳定性。

Conclusion: 为正交群作用下轨道闭包的K-理论分类提供了更具体的计算工具，揭示了展开系数的稳定性质。

Abstract: The orthogonal and symplectic groups act on the complete flag variety with finitely many orbits. The orthogonal Grothendieck polynomials $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{O}}_z$ and symplectic Grothendieck polynomials $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{Sp}}_z$ are distinguished representatives for the $K$-theory classes of the corresponding orbit closures. There is a simple formula to expand $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{Sp}}_z$ as a linear combination of Grothendieck polynomials $\mathfrak{G}^{(β)}_w$, which represent the $K$-theory classes of Schubert varieties. Although the constructions of $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{Sp}}_z$ and $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{O}}_z$ are similar, finding the $\mathfrak{G}^{(β)}$-expansion of $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{O}}_z$ or even computing $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{O}}_z$ is much harder. If $z$ is vexillary then $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{O}}_z$ has a nonnegative $\mathfrak{G}^{(β)}$-expansion, but the associated coefficients are mostly unknown. This paper derives several new formulas for $\mathfrak{G}^{\mathsf{O}}_z$ and its $\mathfrak{G}^{(β)}$-expansion when $z$ is vexillary. Among other applications, we prove that the latter expansion has a nontrivial stability property.

</details>
