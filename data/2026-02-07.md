### [2] [Estimating the spectral radius of Bell-type operator via finite dimensional approximation of orthogonal projections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.10939)
*Yuki Fujii,Toyohiro Tsurumaru*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 论文建立了两个正交投影P和Q在可分希尔伯特空间上的新分解公式，将空间分解为P和Q的不变子空间，每个子空间要么最多二维要么无限维。在无限维分量上，投影对具有"单移位形式"的矩阵表示，可用于从有限维结果推导无限维结果。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究两个正交投影在无限维希尔伯特空间中的结构，为分析涉及两个正交投影的各种问题提供统一框架，特别是在量子力学中与贝尔-CHSH算子相关的谱半径估计问题。

Method: 建立正交投影P和Q的新分解公式，将空间分解为不变子空间；在无限维分量上引入"单移位形式"的矩阵表示；通过有限维子空间上的正交投影来近似无限维投影。

Result: 获得了P和Q的分解公式，证明了在无限维分量上存在单移位形式的矩阵表示；给出了交换子[P,Q]谱半径的上界和下界估计，当投影对具有"常角单移位形式"时这些界是精确的。

Abstract: We establish a new decomposition formula for two orthogonal projections P and Q on a separable Hilbert space V. This formula yields an orthogonal direct sum decomposition of V into invariant subspaces under P and Q, each of which is either at most two dimensional or infinite dimensional. On every infinite dimensional component, the pair (P,Q) admits a matrix representation that we call the "one-shifted form". This representation diagonalizes both P and Q into blocks of size at most two, and moreover, both projections can be explicitly approximated by orthogonal projections on finite dimensional subspaces. This approximation scheme offers a way to derive infinite dimensional results from their finite dimensional counterparts and is also useful in numerical computations.

In particular, several spectral problems for operators generated by P and Q (including polynomials in P and Q) can be reduced to the case where the pair admits a one-shifted form.
  More concretely, we can estimate the spectral radius of [P,Q], which is equivalent to estimating the spectral radius of the Bell-CHSH operator, a quantity of fundamental importance in quantum mechanics. We provide an upper bound and a lower bound for the spectral radius of [P,Q], which become exact when the matrix representations of P and Q are in "constant-angle one-shifted form".

### [5] [Algebraic Geometry for Spin-Adapted Coupled Cluster Theory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16646)
*Fabian M. Faulstich,Svala Sverrisdóttir*

Main category: physics.chem-ph

TL;DR: 开发了自旋适应耦合簇理论的代数几何框架，通过识别SU(2)不变多体空间与Artinian交换环，显著降低了维度、度数和计算复杂度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 由于电子哈密顿量具有SU(2)不变性，物理相关的量子态位于自旋单重态扇区。传统自旋广义方法存在维度冗余，需要开发自旋适应的代数几何框架来减少计算复杂度。

Method: 将SU(2)不变多体空间识别为Artinian交换环（激发环），其维度由Narayana数控制。通过嵌入该环的分级子空间定义自旋适应截断簇，并将CCS截断簇识别为Grassmannian的Veronese平方。

Result: 相比自旋广义公式，该方法在维度和度数上实现显著减少，截断簇的CC度数降低数个数量级。缩放研究显示渐近改进，并成功计算了水和氢化锂的自旋适应CC方程完整解景观。

Conclusion: 自旋适应耦合簇理论的代数几何框架通过利用SU(2)对称性，大幅降低了计算复杂度，为精确量子化学计算提供了更高效的方法，特别是在计算完整解景观方面具有优势。

Abstract: We develop and numerically analyze an algebraic-geometric framework for spin-adapted coupled-cluster (CC) theory. Since the electronic Hamiltonian is SU(2)-invariant, physically relevant quantum states lie in the spin singlet sector. We give an explicit description of the SU(2)-invariant (spin singlet) many-body space by identifying it with an Artinian commutative ring, called the excitation ring, whose dimension is governed by a Narayana number. We define spin-adapted truncation varieties via embeddings of graded subspaces of this ring, and we identify the CCS truncation variety with the Veronese square of the Grassmannian. Compared to the spin-generalized formulation, this approach yields a substantial reduction in dimension and degree, with direct computational consequences. In particular, the CC degree of the truncation variety -- governing the number of homotopy paths required to compute all CC solutions -- is reduced by orders of magnitude. We present scaling studies demonstrating asymptotic improvements and we exploit this reduction to compute the full solution landscape of spin-adapted CC equations for water and lithium hydride.
