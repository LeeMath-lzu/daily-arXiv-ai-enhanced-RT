### [1] [Center-preserving irreducible representations of finite groups](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15266)
*Pierre-Emmanuel Caprace,Geoffrey Janssens,François Thilmany*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 证明了如果有限群H有忠实不可约表示ρ，则诱导表示Ind_H^G(ρ)的某个不可约分量在H上是中心保持的，从而建立了H有忠实不可约表示的充要条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究有限群表示理论中的中心保持性质，探索忠实不可约表示的存在性与诱导表示性质之间的关系。

Method: 通过群表示理论的方法，特别是诱导表示和不可约分解的技术，分析中心保持表示的性质，并构造相关例子。

Result: 证明了如果H有忠实不可约表示ρ，则Ind_H^G(ρ)的某个不可约分量在H上是中心保持的；建立了H有忠实不可约表示的充要条件。

Conclusion: 该结果为有限群表示理论提供了新的视角，揭示了忠实不可约表示与中心保持性质之间的深刻联系，并讨论了与射影表示的关系。

Abstract: Given finite groups $H \leq G$, a representation $σ$ of $G$ is called center-preserving on $H$ if the only elements of $H$ that become central under $σ$ are those that were already central in $G$. We prove that if $H$ has a faithful irreducible representation $ρ$, then at least one of the irreducible components of the induction $\operatorname{Ind}_H^G(ρ)$ is center-preserving on $H$. In consequence, $H$ has a faithful irreducible representation if and only if every finite group $G$ containing $H$ as a subgroup has an irreducible representation whose restriction to $H$ is faithful, and which is center-preserving on $H$. In addition, we give examples illustrating the sharpness of the statement, and discuss the connection with projective representations.

Main category: math.AC

Motivation: 研究Cohen-Macaulay完全局部环的支配指数，探索在奇点范畴中构建剩余域所需的最小扩展次数，建立不同类型环的支配指数上界。

Method: 使用奇点范畴理论，通过代数几何和同调代数方法，分析不同类别环的结构特性，推导支配指数的上界估计。

Abstract: Let R be a d-dimensional Cohen-Macaulay complete local ring with infinite residue field k.
