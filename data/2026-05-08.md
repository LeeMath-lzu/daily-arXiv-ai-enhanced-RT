### [1] [Equivariant version of the characteristic quasi-polynomials of root systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05695)
*Ryo Uchiumi*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文定义了根系的等变特征拟多项式（包含置换特征标），证明了其在等变K-理论下的若干性质，显式计算了所有不可约既约根系的情形，并探讨了与扩展Dynkin图折叠构造的根系之间的关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统特征拟多项式只给出计数信息，缺乏群作用下的精细结构。引入等变版本（含置换特征标）可刻画Coxeter排列模q补集上的对称性，是经典理论的自然推广。

Method: 采用等变K-理论框架，对不可约既约根系分类计算等变特征拟多项式；利用扩展Dynkin图折叠技术构造新根系并研究其等变拟多项式的关系。

Result: 给出了所有不可约既约根系的等变特征拟多项式的显式公式；揭示了通过折叠扩展Dynkin图得到的根系与该等变拟多项式之间的结构联系。

Conclusion: 等变特征拟多项式是对经典理论的精细推广，通过等变K-理论获得了更丰富的对称性信息，且与扩展Dynkin图折叠构造存在深刻关联。

Abstract: An equivariant characteristic quasi-polynomial is a quasi-polynomial in $q$ consisting of the permutation character on the mod $q$ complement of the corresponding Coxeter arrangement. This concept is a refinement of the conventional characteristic quasi-polynomials of root systems. In this paper, we will show equivariant-theoretic refinements of the some properties of characteristic quasi-polynomials of root systems. Furthermore, we will explicitly compute equivariant characteristic quasi-polynomials of all irreducible reduced root systems and discuss the relationship with root systems constructed by the folding of the extended Dynkin diagrams.

### [2] [Stable Invariants of Words from Random Matrices](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.17733)
*Doron Puder,Yotam Shomroni,Danielle Ernst-West,Matan Seidel*

Main category: math.GR

TL;DR: 本文形式化定义了从词在随机矩阵中产生的拓扑不变量，揭示了这些不变量与稳定换位子长度（scl）的类比关系，并在广义对称群情形下给出部分答案，同时提出一系列猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索从词在随机幺正矩阵的傅里叶系数中定义的稳定换位子长度（scl）是否可以推广到随机置换、随机正交矩阵等情形，产生新的词不变量，并研究这些不变量是否具有内在的拓扑描述。

Method: 形式化定义从词在各类随机矩阵中产生的不变量；在广义对称群情形下计算并回答相关问题；对其他情形提出详细猜想。

Result: 在广义对称群情形下给出了部分答案，发现这些不变量与Wilton最近定义的稳定本原秩和非定向scl类比等不变量相关。

Conclusion: 这些由随机矩阵衍生的词不变量是新的且有趣的，并在某些情形下具有拓扑、组合和代数的内在描述，但大量问题仍需进一步研究。

Abstract: Let $w$ be a word in a free group. A few years ago, Magee and the first named author discovered that the stable commutator length (scl) of $w$, a well-known topological invariant, can also be defined in terms of certain Fourier coefficients of $w$-random unitary matrices [arXiv:1802.04862]. But the random-matrix side of this equality can be naturally tweaked by considering $w$-random permutations, $w$-random orthogonal matrices and so on, to produce new invariants for any given word. Are these invariants new? interesting? Do they admit an intrinsic topological description as in the case of $w$-random unitaries and scl?
  The current paper formalizes the definition of these invariants coming from $w$-random matrices, answers the above questions in certain cases involving generalized symmetric groups, and poses detailed conjectures in many others. In particular, we present a plethora of topological, combinatorial and algebraic invariants of words which play, or are at least conjectured to play, a similar role to the one played by scl in the above-mentioned result. Among others, these invariants include two invariants recently defined by Wilton [arXiv:2210.09853]: the stable primitivity rank and a non-oriented analog of scl.

### [3] [A Sugawara-Legendre mechanism for the hyperelliptic Heisenberg algebra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06090)
*Felipe Albino dos Santos*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文研究Heisenberg子代数$\mathcal{H}_m$的$\varphi$-Verma模，证明在超椭圆情形$m=2$时：Shapovalov型在Legendre多项式基下对角化且范数为$2/(2n+1)$；模不可约当且仅当$\varphi$是$p$-可容许的；存在显式交织算子将Sugawara零模映为Legendre微分算子，将最高权向量映为Legendre多项式。对于$m\ge 3$的情形以猜想形式给出。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究超椭圆曲线上$\mathfrak{sl}_2\otimes A_m$通用中心扩张的Heisenberg子代数$\mathcal{H}_m$的$\varphi$-Verma模理论如何控制中心关系中出现的正交多项式族，建立表示论与经典特殊函数之间的深层联系。

Method: 通过构造$\varphi$-Verma模$M(\varphi)$上的Shapovalov型，在由cocycle确定的多项式基$\{\tilde{P}_n\}_{n\ge 0}$下计算其对角化形式；定义$p$-可容许性条件判定模的不可约性；构造从$M(\varphi)$到$\mathbb{C}[x]$的显式交织算子$\Phi$。

Result: 超椭圆情形下三条定理：(1) Shapovalov型在多项式基下对角，Legendre平方范数$h_n=2/(2n+1)$；(2) $M(\varphi)$不可约$\iff$$\varphi$是$p$-可容许的$\iff$Shapovalov型非退化；(3) 交织算子将Sugawara零模$\Omega$映为Legendre微分算子$L$，最高权向量映为Legendre多项式$P_n(x)$，Casimir塔$\{\Omega^r\}$映为$\{L^r\}$。且$M(\varphi)$典范同构于玻色Fock空间，Shapovalov型与Fock内积一致。

Conclusion: 在超椭圆情形完整建立了$\mathcal{H}_m$的Verma模理论与Legendre正交多项式之间的对应关系，揭示了表示论中Shapovalov型、不可约性判别与经典特殊函数理论的深层统一。将$m\ge 3$的推广列为猜想，为后续研究提供了方向。

Abstract: We study the $\varphi$-Verma modules of the Heisenberg subalgebra $\mathcal{H}_m$ of the universal central extension of $\mathfrak{sl}_2 \otimes A_m$, where $A_m$ is the coordinate ring of the superelliptic curve $u^m = P(t)$, and ask how the orthogonal polynomial families that arise in the centre relations are controlled by the module theory of $\mathcal{H}_m$. Our main results are proved unconditionally for the hyperelliptic case $m=2$, $r=1$; corresponding statements for $m \ge 3$ are recorded as conjectures. In the hyperelliptic case we prove three theorems. First, the canonical contravariant (Shapovalov) form on $M(\varphi)$ is diagonal in the polynomial basis $\{\tilde{P}_n\}_{n \ge 0}$ determined by the cocycle, with Legendre squared norms $h_n = 2/(2n+1)$. Second, $M(\varphi)$ is irreducible if and only if $\varphi$ is $p$-admissible, and this is equivalent to non-degeneracy of the Shapovalov form. Third, there is an explicit intertwiner $Φ\colon M(\varphi) \to \mathbb{C}[x]$ which sends the free-boson Sugawara zero mode $Ω= -L_0(L_0 + \mathrm{Id}) \in \widetilde{U(\mathcal{H}_m)}$ to the classical Legendre differential operator $L = (1-x^2)\partial_x^2 - 2x\partial_x$, the level-$n$ image of the highest-weight vector to the Legendre polynomial $P_n(x)$, and the Casimir tower $\{Ω^r\}_{r \ge 1}$ to $\{L^r\}_{r \ge 1}$. As a companion result, $M(\varphi)$ is canonically isomorphic to a bosonic Fock space with the Shapovalov form identified with the Fock inner product.
