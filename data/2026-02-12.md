### [1] [A non-semisimple Witt class](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10519)
*Victor Ostrik,Alexandra Utiralova*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文描述了无限族辫子有限张量范畴，其中最简单例子给出了一个非退化辫子张量范畴，该范畴不与半单范畴Witt等价。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究辫子张量范畴的分类和性质，特别是寻找非退化且不与半单范畴Witt等价的例子，以丰富辫子张量范畴的理论结构。

Method: 构造了多个无限族的辫子有限张量范畴，通过系统性的数学构造方法生成这些范畴。

Result: 成功构造了无限族辫子有限张量范畴，其中最简单例子是一个非退化辫子张量范畴，且该范畴不与任何半单范畴Witt等价。

Conclusion: 该工作扩展了辫子张量范畴的分类，提供了新的非退化且不与半单范畴Witt等价的例子，对辫子张量范畴理论有重要意义。

Abstract: We describe several infinite families of braided finite tensor categories. A simplest example gives a non-degenerate braided tensor category which is not Witt equivalent to a semisimple category.

</details>


### [2] [Little $q$-Jacobi polynomials and symmetry breaking operators for $U_q(sl_2)$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.23848)
*Quentin Labriet,Loïc Poulain d'Andecy*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 该论文给出了量子群U_q(sl_2)在Verma模张量积上作用的显式公式，将全息算子表示为小q-Jacobi多项式，将对称破缺算子表示为Rankin-Cohen算子的q-变形，并建立了与q-Hahn多项式表示的Clebsch-Gordan系数的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子群U_q(sl_2)在Verma模张量积上的交织算子，旨在获得这些算子的显式公式，并建立与特殊函数和已知数学结构的联系。

Method: 将Verma模实现在多项式空间上，使用非交换变量，通过小q-Jacobi多项式表达全息算子，通过q-变形Rankin-Cohen算子表达对称破缺算子。

Result: 获得了交织算子的显式公式，建立了全息算子与小q-Jacobi多项式的联系，对称破缺算子与q-变形Rankin-Cohen算子的联系，以及与q-Hahn多项式表示的Clebsch-Gordan系数的显式连接。

Conclusion: 成功推导了量子群U_q(sl_2)在Verma模张量积上交织算子的显式公式，揭示了这些算子与特殊函数（小q-Jacobi多项式、q-Hahn多项式）和经典算子（Rankin-Cohen算子）的深刻联系。

Abstract: This paper presents explicit formulas for intertwining operators of the quantum group $U_q(sl_2)$ acting on tensor products of Verma modules. We express a first set of intertwining operators (the holographic operators) in terms of the little $q$-Jacobi polynomials, and we obtain for the dual set (the symmetry breaking operators) a $q$-deformation of the Rankin--Cohen operators. The Verma modules are realised on polynomial spaces and, interestingly, we find along the way the need to work with non-commuting variables. Explicit connections are given with the Clebsch--Gordan coefficients of $U_q(sl_2)$ expressed with the $q$-Hahn polynomials.

</details>

### [3] [Universal TT- and TQ-relations via centrally extended q-Onsager algebra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.15876)
*Pascal Baseilhac,Azat M. Gainutdinov,Guillaume Lemarthe*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文研究了q-Onsager代数的交替中心扩展A_q，建立了自旋-j K算子与已知K矩阵的对应关系，构造了通用转移矩阵并推导了融合层次结构（TT关系），应用于自旋链模型得到局部守恒量的显式计算算法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究q-Onsager代数A_q的表示理论，建立通用框架来处理具有一般可积边界条件的自旋链模型，推导显式的TT关系并计算局部守恒量，为广义吉布斯系综构造提供理论基础。

Method: 分类A_q的一维表示；证明自旋-j K算子与已知K矩阵的等价性；构造通用转移矩阵生成交换子代数；推导通用TT关系（融合层次）；将通用结果应用于自旋链表示得到显式TT关系；利用TT关系计算局部守恒量并建立算法。

Result: 建立了自旋-j K算子与K矩阵的对应关系；推导了通用TT关系；得到了任意自旋值、任意非均匀性和一般边界条件下的显式TT关系；给出了局部守恒量的多项式表达式和显式计算算法；发现了特殊边界条件下的对称性；提出了通用T系统、Y系统和TQ关系。

Conclusion: 该工作为q-Onsager代数A_q建立了完整的通用可积性框架，统一处理了各种自旋链模型，推导了显式的TT关系和局部守恒量计算方法，为广义吉布斯系综构造提供了理论基础，并将结果推广到退化版本的中心扩展增强q-Onsager代数。

Abstract: Let $A_q$ be the alternating central extension of the q-Onsager algebra, a comodule algebra over the quantum loop algebra of $sl_2$. We classify one-dimensional representations of $A_q$, and show that spin-j K-operators constructed in arXiv:2301.00781 act as K-matrices previously obtained in the literature. Using these K-operators and K-matrices, we construct universal spin-j transfer matrices generating commutative subalgebras in $A_q$. Within a technical conjecture, we derive their fusion hierarchy, the so-called universal TT-relations. On spin-chain representations of $A_q$, we show how the universal transfer matrices evaluate to spin-chain transfer matrices, and as a result we get explicit TT-relations for all values of spins for auxiliary and quantum spaces, any inhomogeneities, and general integrable boundary conditions. In particular, we derive previously conjectured TT-relations. Using the TT-relations, we show that n-th local conserved quantities of the spin-j chains of length N are polynomials of total degree 4Njn in two non-local operators of the q-Onsager algebra. As a result, we give an algorithm of explicit calculation of all local conserved quantities in terms of spin operators. Furthermore, using the universal TT-relations we derive exchange relations between spin-j Hamiltonians and the two non-local operators showing non-trivial symmetries for special boundary conditions, that they commute with all Hamiltonian densities. As another application of our universal TT-relations we propose universal T-system, Y-system and universal TQ-relations for $A_q$, and as a result, universal TQ for the q-Onsager algebra. For diagonal boundary conditions, we also obtain universal TT- and TQ-relations for a degenerate version of $A_q$ known as centrally extended augmented q-Onsager algebra. We finally discuss implications of our results for generalized Gibbs ensemble construction.

</details>

### [4] [Iterated Hopf Ore Extensions over Group Rings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10850)
*Can Hatipoğlu,Christian Lomp*

Main category: math.RA

TL;DR: 该论文引入并研究了一类Hopf代数H(G,χ,η,b,c,β)，它们是群代数K[G]的两步Ore扩张，统一并推广了广义Taft代数和Wang、Wu、Tan构造的与sl2相关的Hopf代数等已知族类。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 构建一个统一的框架来研究一类特定的Hopf代数，这些代数能够概括和推广多个已知的重要Hopf代数族类，包括广义Taft代数和与sl2相关的Hopf代数。

Method: 通过两步Ore扩张构造H(G,χ,η,b,c,β)类Hopf代数，其中参数包括群G、特征χ、η等。然后分析这些代数的环论性质，并分类其所有有限维单模。

Result: 成功构造了一类统一的Hopf代数，分析了其环论性质，并完成了所有有限维单模的完全分类。

Conclusion: 该研究提供了一个统一的框架，将多个已知的Hopf代数族类纳入同一构造中，并系统地研究了其表示理论，为相关代数结构的研究提供了新的视角和工具。

Abstract: We introduce and study a class of Hopf algebras $H(G, χ, η, b, c, β)$ which are two-step Ore extensions of a group algebra $\mathbb{K}[G]$. This construction unifies and generalizes some known families of Hopf algebras such as generalized Taft algebras and Hopf algebras related to $\mathfrak{sl}_2$ constructed by Wang, Wu, and Tan. We analyze the ring theoretical properties of these algebras and classify all finite dimensional simple modules over them.

</details>
