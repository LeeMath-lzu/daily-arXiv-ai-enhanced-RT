  <summary>Details</summary>

Abstract: We characterize Cohen--Macaulay and $\varphi$-rational perfect schemes in terms of their perverse étale mod $p$ sheaves. <details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将有限长度性质从可数纯集、可数稠密线性序推广到更一般的无限结构。

Method: 通过有限子结构近似和自由融合的Fraïssé极限两种方法进行推广证明。

Abstract: An infinite structure has the finite length property (over a given field) if, for each of its finite powers, chains of equivariant subspaces in the corresponding free vector space are bounded in length.
